The first is located on a raised platform east of Reliance. To reach it you need to take a vehicle of your own to the base of Knotty Peak. There you will find a small piece of metal sticking out over the cliff-edge. Use this metal to propel your vehicle through the air so that it lands on the platform next to your Hijack Target. The vehicle of choice for this stunt is a Cyclone, because it won’t take that much room up on the platform, preventing you from being accidentally knocked off. Before performing the jump, it’s a good idea to line up your car before making the jump. This will allow to pick up speed and then, as you leave the platform, hit the accelerator to ensure you get enough height to reach the platform. The first is located in the far eastern corner of Desolation’s Edge, near the Catch-A-Ride station that sits between the two ruins. The first thing you’ll want to do is take out the monsters that make their home in this area. Once they’re dead use your melee attack to break the vines that lie between you and the Hijack Target. The second Hijack Target can be found in the Maliwan camp in Desolation’s Edge. When you reach the first section of the Maliwan camp, head to the right-hand side and use the stairs to take you into a new part of the camp. You’ll want to kill all the attacking Maliwan forces in this area, before dealing with the Hijack Target. Once all the Maliwan soldiers are dead, you’ll notice that the shield protecting the target has three separate wires powering it. We’ll go from left to right. The first wire, the one on the left-hand side, will lead you to a small generator just next to the building containing the vehicle. Shoot this generator to destroy it. The second wire, the one in the middle, leads to a generator in the middle of the camp. Shoot this one too. The third wire, the one on the right-hand side, is on top of a tower opposite the building containing the vehicle. You can easily blow it up with a gun from the ground, especially if you use a sniper rifle. Do this quickly or the generators will come back online. When the shield goes down, you’ll be able to hop into the vehicle and take it to the nearest Catch-A-Ride. Hijacking this vehicle will reward you with the Energy Cell for the Outrunner and:

7,473 XP $841 20 Eridium
