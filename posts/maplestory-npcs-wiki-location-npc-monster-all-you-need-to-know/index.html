There are thousands of fans who have recently been searching for MapleStory NPCs and if that’s you then look no further as we have got you everything covered here. All MapleStory NPCs that we have enlisted here have been grouped by town or major area (such as an island). Since there are numerous NPCs are available in MapleStory, use Ctrl+F to find the desired one.

MapleStory NPCs Appearing In Multiple Towns#

Cassandra#

Location: Ariant, Henesys, Magatia, Mushroom,  Shrine, Rienm and Sleepywood Note: It is also known as “Agent Cassandra” as part of the “Find Master M” event.

Catherine#

Location: Aquarium, Ariant, Ellinia, El Nath, Henesys, Orbis, Magatia, Mu Lung, Mushroom Shrine,  Nautilus Harbor, and Perion Note: You can use it to get teleported to Pachinko Parlor (JapanMS only).

Cody Wizet Wizard#

Location: Henesys, Ellinia, Perion, Kerning City, Lith Harbor, Orbis, El Nath, Ludibrium, Leafre, Edelstein, and NLC Town Center Note: It deals with events.

Dalair Monk of Honor#

Location: El Nath, Henesys, and Kerning City. Note: It accompanies the Donation Box. Assigns title quests.

Dimensional Mirror#

Location: Aquarium, Ariant, CBD, Edelstein, Ellinia, El Nath, Henesys, Herb Town, Kerning City, Korean Folk Town, Leafre, Lith Harbor, Ludibrium, Magatia, Mu Lung, Mushroom Shrine, Nautilus Harbor, NLC Town Center, Omega Sector, Orbis Park, Perion, and Sleepywood Note: It will help you get teleported to the entry point of a Party Quest or area. When you return through that door, it will head back to the city you were in. Depending on your level, the Mirror of Dimension will bring you the following areas: Pyramid’s Net Ghost Ship in the Sea Mist Mu Lung Dojo Fight for Azwan NLC (GMS Only) Battle Mode (PVP)

How Do I Find A Specific Location, NPC, or Monster?#

There are multiple ways that can be used to find a specific location, NPC and Monster in MapleStory. In this post, we will share with you three methods to locate NPC and Monster in MapleStory.

Method 1#

Launch MapleStory and open up your World Map. Hover over a map icon. When you do so, it will bring a list of Monsters and NPCs available in that map.

Method 2#

Open MapleStory and search for a specific map, monsters, and NPCs. The result you will get based on your level. Once you have searched, double-click a result to see its location.

Method 3#

There are some quests in MapleStory that have an option to find NPC. Click on that button to locate NPCs that are involved in that quest. Notably, there are some NPCs that can’t be found using this method.

These are some MapleStory NPCs available in the game right now. There are numerous NPCs that are available in MapleStory yet to be updated here. We will soon update each and every MapleStory NPCs here.
