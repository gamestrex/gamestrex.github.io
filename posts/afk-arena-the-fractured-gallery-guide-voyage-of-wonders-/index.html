AFK Arena – The Fractured Gallery#

The Fractured Gallery is the next best realm of the famous action RPG title AFK Arena, and trust me when I quote this “the elements featured in this realm are brand-new versus the elements incorporated in the previous events. To grab the special rewards of the event you will be making 3 trips through the Fractured Gallery. The rewards, hero recommendations, and Steps are provided in the next section so keep up with me until the end.

Quick Overview#

To enter the final phases of the event, you will travel between the islands. When solving the Fractured Gallery Event in AFK Arena, you will be mandated to take debuffs along the route in addition to fight the enemies roaming the islands. You will discover several Golden Chests between the islands that are yours to claim. Also Read | AFK Arena Twisted Realm Guide To proceed to the next island, you will need to use the portal. Essentially, the reason for making three runs to claim the prizes is an inability to return to the island. Let’s assume you figure out a way to return, but it’ll be a terrible idea because debuffs will chew you alive at a certain point.

Rewards#

The rewards you obtain once the Fractured Gallery event in AFK Arena concludes are listed below:

1x Hero Choice Chest 10x Faction Emblem Choice Chest 10x Common Hero Scroll 360x Rare Hero Soulstones 20x Yellow Emblems 20x Purple Emblems Gold Booster EXP Booster Essence Booster

Hero Selection#

Sadly or happily for Hero Choice Chest in the AFK Arena’s Fractured Gallery realm, you will have an option to select between 10X Faction rolls or any one of the below heroes:

Sauras Nara Vurk Oscar Flora

Walkthrough#

The Fractured Gallery event in AFK Arena requires 3 runs to clear. Scroll to see how to finish the 3 runs successfully.

Run 1#

Here’s the step for completing 1st run:

Step 1: Start the event and have a conversation with Skreg and Vurk. Step 2: You will get the 1st portal choice. In addition, you will also get the Hero Choice Chest. Ensure you select the left island for the first 3 times. Step 3: You will be thrown to the huge middle island. Here clear all the camps and chests to the right. Step 4: After clearing through all the camps and chest to the right in step 3, use the left portal and hit the next island. Step 5: Jump along with the small islands. Here you will be given just 1 portal choice for the final island. Pick the Right island. Step 6: You will be landed upon the last island featuring a Crystal Chest. Step 7: Win the boss battle and grab the chest to complete the first run.

Run 2#

Here’s the step for completing 2nd run:

Step 1: Start again. Select the Right island 2 times. Step 2: Select the Left Island one time. Step 3: Again select the Left island one time. Step 4: You will hit the middle island unlike the one in run 1. Step 5: Clear all the camps and chest to the left. Step 6: Use the left portal and proceed to the next island.

Step 7: Now select right. Step 8: You will be taken to the other side of the middle island. Clear all the camps and chests however don’t use the portal yet. Step 9: Walk down and use the portal from step 6. Step 10: Again select the right option. Step 11: You will be directed to a small island. Now choose left. Step 12: Now to hit the final island keep choosing the next islands one by one until you reach your destination.

With this, your 2nd run for the Fractured Gallery in AFK Arena ends here. Now it’s time for the last and final run of the event.

Run 3#

Here’s the step for completing 3rd run:

Step 1: The final run is the same as 2nd run the only difference is you will have to click on the Right 3 times. Step 2: You will reach the same middle island as in the 2nd run however you can take the last chest this time on your way. Step 3: Take the left portal. Step 4: Select the Left option 2 times. Step 5: Collect the last remaining Golden Chest.

Finally, after 3 thrilling runs, the Fractured Gallery quest in AFK Arena ends and you earn unique rewards. Hopefully, this guide comes in handy to you, Until next time stays tuned.
