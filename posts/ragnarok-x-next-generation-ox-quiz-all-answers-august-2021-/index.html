How To Start Ragnarok X Next Generation OX Quiz, Schedule, Eligibility#

Players need to be at least level 21 to be able to play the OX quiz. The OX quiz happens three times every day in Ragnarok X Next Generation. The OX Quiz is available from 12 pm to 2 pm, 6:30 pm to 8:30 pm and 10 pm to 12 am. There are 2 ways players can start the OX quiz either from the Carnival or the Prontera square. Players can head to the Prontera square and then talk to Oxi, the registrar of the OX quiz to start the quiz or you can just head to the OX quiz stand in the Carnival.
