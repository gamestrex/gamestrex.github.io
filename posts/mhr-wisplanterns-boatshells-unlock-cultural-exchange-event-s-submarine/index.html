Monster Hunter Rise – Wisplanterns & Boatshells Locations#

To successfully conclude the Cultural Exchange Event and obtain the Submarine in Monster Hunter Rise the most required items as mentioned above are Wisplanterns and Boatshells. These important resources are located in two different maps meaning your character will have to do some leg work and search for them in the two maps. Now, where exactly are the Oysters situated in Frost Island? to the middle and eastern parts of the map.
