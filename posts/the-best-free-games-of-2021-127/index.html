For all of you looking for free-to-play gacha games, the tower defense game Arknights is the easiest one to recommend. Each character has a specific niche in the meta, and the game regularly releases new types of challenge maps to keep each niche relevant. Some characters are great for blocking foes. Others have assassins, or they are good at a quick-drop attack. There is virtually no single strategy that is applied to a few hard maps. If you’ve always wanted to watch games like Fire Emblem, you’d better pick the ones who have fun in Arknights. Alchemy Stars#

This team-based strategy game was the greatest hit of 2021. Alchemy Stars has beautiful character art, the best mobile games soundtrack, and a fun turn-based combat system. Your character moves on a grid-based map, and you match a color and color to execute special combinations. Do you give priority to attacking multiple enemies at the same time? Do you focus your attacks on one boss? Do you run or hit? Do you take over enemy range to execute several devastating combat combos next turn? Alchemy Stars gameplay is easy to master, but still hard to play. The game is often updated. I can’t remember a single lull in which there wasn’t an ongoing event. The story is accurate, and I’d better put it on the same level as Arknights. Nevertheless, Alchemy Stars also suffers from localization problems. The translations make it easier for me to understand the story, but I wish they were more elegantly localized. If the story isn’t the best dealbreaker for you, Alchemy Stars is one of the best strategy games on your mobile device.
