Best product on Raspberry Pi – Raspberry Pi Pico.#

In the decade that it existed, Raspberry Pi has changed the face of computing and the maker movement, but the company behind everyones favorite single-board computers has never been as disruptive as it has in 2021. This year, the company released the first piece of custom silicone, the RP2040, a dual-core, 133 MHz – SoC that powers a whole slew of microcontrollers that compete with the output from many industry heavyweights like Arduino and NXP. Though you can find RP2040 chips on dozens of third-party products, the Raspberry Pi Pico is the first model that started the series of four years ago when it was released in January last year. It comes with 26 GPIO-bails, three ADCs, 2 SPI-battles, 2 I2C-battles, two UART-Battles, and a total of 8 PIO-caching-battles. The $4 Raspberry Pi Pico isn’t a Linux computer, but by careful engineering, the RP2040 can emulate retro computers, drive HDMI displays and train AI projects with machine learning. This is the first silicon from the Raspberry Pi, but due to the success of the Raspberry Pi Pi Pico and the many RP2040-based alternatives, it won’t become the last.
