MGCM Magical Girls: Beginners Guide & Tips#

Here’s an MGCM Magical Girls full beginner’s guide and some tips for you.

Rewards for Newbies

As being a newbie, if you have just started playing the game, then the game will generally give you a lot of Gems. You can use these Gems to buy the Gacha’s in the game, or you can just draw a Bundle of 10x and try to acquire SR and above characters. The game tends to give a lot of rewards to the new accounts. So just don’t waste your time and redeem the gems as soon as you can.

Choose Wisely

After redeeming your Gems and acquiring the best Magical Girls. You just need to place them in your deck and make your team stronger. If you’re confused about which character you should equip, don’t worry just follow these steps.

For placing the most optimized party automatically, just go to the Dress option. Then just click on the Quest Deck. After that click on the Remove all option. Then just click on Recommended, and there you are.

Note: Don’t forget to save your deck.

Focus on the Main Story

The most important thing you need to do is, to go through the story and get to know every basic thing about the game. Going through the story and every basic information will help you unlock features quickly. To save your time we have listed every basic detail and the story below, just go through it and you’ll be good to go.

Story & Basic Details#

Story and characters list

In MGCM Magical Girls, the player plays the lead character of the story and manages a team of Magical Girls. As the lead character of the story, the main job of the player is to fight alongside the Magical Girls and guide them through the journey. As you keep progressing in the story, you’ll meet more Magical Girls. The player’s main aim should be, to strengthen their bonds with the girls and destroy the darkness by defeating the demons. Just make sure to choose the best Magical Girl and make your team stronger. There are around 12 Magical Girls for you to choose from and battle alongside them. Here’s a list of all Magical Girls available in the game.

Asobe Iroha Tomonaga Kaori Sodeshiro Seira Yuwase Cocoa Higashiyama Akisa Ohtori Ao Ohtori Aka Yukifune Eliza Yarimizu Lilly Tamaki Hanabi Okada Marianne Oikawa Iko Bond Level

Bond is one of the important things in MGCM Magical Girls. These Bond levels show how much your relationship with the Magical Girls has grown. To increase your bond with the Magical Girls, you just need to clear the stages by fighting alongside them. As a bonus, whenever you raise your Bond Level with the Magical Girls, you will receive a reward.

Dress

Dress is the costumes that your Magical Girls can equip. Every dress contains different elements and abilities. You can also upgrade these abilities with your Magical Silks and Bells. The player can pick one dress as their main dress and 4 different dresses for the Magical Girls. Every sub-dresses buffs the character stats and makes the player stronger. These dresses come in different types of rarity, such as N (Normal), R (Rare), SR (Super Rare), and UR (Ultra Rare). Although, you can also increase the dress level via the Limit Break option by spending Spheres.

Quests and Battles

In the Quest menu, there are many kinds of quests available in the game. Such as Main Story, Daily Quests, Challenge Quests, Event Quests, Endless Quest, Demon’s Tower, and many more. The player can pick any kind of battles they want to play from these quests. In these battles, you can also pick the skill of your Magical Girls, that they will use against the demons. To increase the winning possibilities the player can also activate certain functions, such as.

Play Speed: Increases the battle speed by 1x, 2x, 3x Auto Play: This function lets the AI take control over the game and plays it for you. Auto pass: This function replays the same stage to make it easy for you to farm rewards. Orbs

Orbs are the items, that players can equip to increase the stats of the character. It works the same as the dress, and also comes in different kinds of rarity. For now, there are 3 types of Orbs available in the game, which are.

Sparkly: Ultra Rare to find Glowing: Super Rare to find Shiny: Rare to find Missions

To gain more rewards, the player has to finish the missions. There are 3 types of missions available in the game.

Beginner Missions Daily Missions Normal Missions

there are also some daily tasks, that the player needs to check regularly in the game.

Elements

There are 5 types of elements available in the game, that your magical girls can equip. Here’s a list of all elements with their weaknesses.

Fire: Weakness – Water Thunder: Weakness – Fire Water: Weakness – Thunder Dark: Weakness – Light Light: Weakness – Dark
