Best Crosshairs in CSGO with Codes#

Having a good crosshair result in effortless kills. It also improves your reaction time. Customized crosshair is often used by players for better aiming as it is different from the background and easier to aim at enemies. So here are some crosshairs and their settings that you should give a try to.

Xantares – Best Crosshairs in CSGO#

Xantares is a Turkish-Macedonian professional gamer who has a headshot percentage of more than 50% in CS: GO. He used a crosshair that had no center dot. This creates a gap between the lines depicting the head of your opponent. This helps to improve your focus and easier to execute a headshot. The color of your crosshair is vibrant green which makes your crosshair clear to see. You can use his settings by copying the following commands in the command tab of the game.

cl_crosshairalpha 255; cl_crosshaircolor 1; cl_crosshaircolor_b 50; cl_crosshaircolor_r 50; cl_crosshaircolor_g 250; cl_crosshairdot 0; cl_crosshairgap 0; cl_crosshairsize 3; cl_crosshairstyle 4; cl_crosshairusealpha 1; cl_crosshairthickness 0.5; cl_crosshair_drawoutline 0;

Hobbit#

Hobbit is an ex CS: GO player from Kazakhstan with good stats in the game. He used crosshair that has balanced visibility and was very thick in size. This approach had the disadvantage of more distraction from the background. This demerit narrows its fans as it requires lots of focus. For using this crosshair you can copy the below settings in the game command.

cl_crosshairsize 3; cl_crosshairgap -2; cl_crosshairthickness 0.4; cl_crosshaircolor 5; cl_crosshaircolor_b 144; cl_crosshaircolor_g 195; cl_crosshaircolor_r 27; cl_crosshairdot 1; cl_crosshairstyle 4; cl_crosshairalpha 250; cl_crosshairgap_useweaponvalue 0; cl_crosshairusealpha 1; cl_crosshair_drawoutline 0; cl_crosshair_dynamic_maxdist_splitratio 0.35; cl_crosshair_dynamic_splitalpha_innermod 1; cl_crosshair_dynamic_splitalpha_outermod 0.5; cl_crosshair_dynamic_splitdist 7;

Yuurih#

Yuurih is a Brazilian pro player of the game who was ranked 14 in the world rank in 2020 by HLTV and was MVP of the Dreamhack Spring event This crosshair is more compact as compared to other crosshairs offered by the game. This crosshair increases the accuracy of the players as the whole crosshair depicts the head of the opponent which makes it easier for players while shooting at their opponent. The vibrant yellow color of this crosshair also plays a vital role in players focusing on the center of their screen. It also creates a contrast from other colors in the background making it easier to focus on it. If want to use this crosshair here are the settings mentioned below.

cl_crosshairalpha 200; cl_crosshaircolor 2; cl_crosshairdot 0; cl_crosshairgap -3; cl_crosshairsize 3; cl_crosshairstyle 4; cl_crosshairusealpha 1; cl_crosshairthickness 1; cl_crosshair_drawoutline 0;

Niko#

Niko a Bosnian pro gamer is the oldest and best gamer in terms of achievements in the game. He started his gaming career by playing CS 1.6 back in 2011. He is known for his aggressive and dominant with top-class rifling skills. Currently plays for G2. He uses a very small and compact T- shape crosshair. His crosshair is the best pick for players who are comfortable with a small and compact crosshair. His crosshair has a white color that contrasts with the color scheme used in the map, making it suitable for dim displays and low-light areas on the map. Players who want to use this crosshair can use the below-mentioned codes.

cl_crosshairalpha 255; cl_crosshaircolor 5; cl_crosshairdot 0; cl_crosshairgap -4; cl_crosshairsize 1; cl_crosshairstyle 4; cl_crosshairthickness 1; cl_crosshair_drawoutline 0; cl_crosshair_sniper_width 1; cl_crosshaircolor_r 255; cl_crosshaircolor_g 255; cl_crosshaircolor_b 255;

Dev1ce is a Danish former number one player, who started his career with CS: Source he has played many tournaments and has won many prizes in his long career. He uses a fairly small crosshair with yellow color making it attractive. It is less thick than other players’ crosshairs. For players who want to use this crosshair, below are the command.

ZYWOo#

ZYWOo is a French pro player who has become one of the top players in the game at the young age of 21. His main skills are AWP and rifles. His crosshair is the thickest T- shape crosshair a player can use in the game making it almost disappear from the screen. This crosshair is the best pick for players who like to use sniper rifles or weapons with scopes. The unique quality of this crosshair is the gap in the middle of it which is more as compared to other small crosshairs making it comfortable for players who like to peer in from the middle of their crosshair. Here are the commands for this crosshair.

cl_crosshair_drawoutline 1; cl_crosshair_sniper_width 1; cl_crosshairalpha 200; cl_crosshaircolor 1; cl_crosshaircolor_b 50; cl_crosshaircolor_g 250; cl_crosshaircolor_r 50; cl_crosshairdot 0; cl_crosshairgap -2; cl_crosshairsize 1.2; cl_crosshairstyle 4; cl_crosshairthickness 0.5;

Shroud#

Shroud is a Canadian professional gamer and a YouTuber who started his career with CS; GO and then shifted to Battle Royale games. He also plays Valorant. He is considered the best aimers in the gaming world as he can score a headshot from any gun. He used a crosshair that had zero gaps in the middle and had a bright cyan color making it stand out from the background. This crosshair requires fewer efforts as players can place the opponent’s head on the center of the crosshair point. Players can use the commands mentioned below for this crosshair.

cl_crosshaircolor 3; cl_fixedcrosshairgap -2.5; cl_crosshairstyle 1; hud_showtargetid 1;
