What Is PDF?#

PDF (Portable Document Format) was introduced in 1993 to assess files in a way that is independent of application software, hardware, and operating system. It is encoded with PostScript languages and all PDF file embodies a complete description of the flat layout. It was standardized as ISO 32000 in 2008 and no more requires copyright for its execution. PDF Files Reader can cover a range of material alongside flat text and images, like logical structuring items, multimedia content such as metadata and form-fields, layers, vector graphics, videos, and three-dimensional artifacts utilizing U3D or PRC, as well as various other communication protocols.
