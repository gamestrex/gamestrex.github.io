Pok Mon Legends Arceus Pok Dex All Pok Mon Locations In The Hisui Pok Dex Listed

November 12, 2022 · 2 min · 342 words · Joshua Walker

Throughout the Hisui region, you’ll find Pokémon from past generations living alongside Hisuian regional forms and new Pokémon making their first appearance in Legends: Arceus. Below you’ll find all of the Pokémon in the Hisui Pokédex listed in accordance with the National Pokédex and, if you’d like to learn more about the game, visit our Pokémon Legends: Arceus walkthrough. On this page: Unlike Pokédex from past Pokémon games, entries in the Hisui Pokédex are filled by completing a number of Research Tasks. To learn more about this feature, visit our dedicated Research Task guide. Two Pokémon also require you to have save files from Sword and / or Shield and Brilliant Diamond and / or Shining Pearl on your Nintendo Switch. Finally, when you catch a shiny Pokémon, its picture will be recorded in the Hisui Pokédex; you can even switch between the traditional appearance for the Pokémon and its shiny variant. This will help you keep track of which shiny forms you’ve caught and those you haven’t. Keep in mind, though, that a number of Pokémon are shiny locked - you can find those Pokémon listed, along with the shiny odds for Legends: Arceus, in our shiny Pokémon guide. Our Pokémon Legends Arceus walkthrough will help you pick a starter Pokémon and win the Kleavor boss fight. You’re also tasked with filling the Hisui Pokédex by obtaining new evolutions like Kleavor by evolving Scyther, Wyrdeer by evolving Stantler, Sneasler by evolving Hisui Sneasel and Overquil by evolving Hisui Qwilfish. There’s a host of new evolution methods, including ones for the Eevee evolutions. Take the time to complete Requests too, such as The Sea’s Legend, Wurmple’s Evolved! and finding the Wisp locations. Don’t forget to track down every Unown location too. Don’t get to find all of the Unown locations, wisp locations for Spiritomb and, if you’re looking for Manaphy, make sure you complete Request 66 - The Sea’s Legend. If you’d like to learn more about the game, check out our Pokémon Legends: Arceus walkthrough! Good luck completing the Hisui Pokédex!
