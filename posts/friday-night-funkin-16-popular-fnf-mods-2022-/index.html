16 Popular Friday Night Funkin’ Mods#

1. The Holiday Mod#

Friday Night Funkin’ got into the winter festive spirit with this holiday mod. The family comes together for a cute Christmas celebration and even Boyfriend has an invitation to the festivities. It truly is a Christmas miracle. All the main characters i.e. Boyfriend, Girlfriend, Mommy Dearest, and Daddy Dearest get a holiday-themed makeover with this mod. Enjoy the classic Friday Night Funkin’ with the festive cheer thanks to this mod.

#

2. FNF vs Mokey the Mouse Mod#

This popular Friday Night Funkin’ mod pits the Boyfriend against Mokey the mouse and friends. Rap it out with this parody version of Disney’s most famous mouse and move one step closer to impressing GF’s family. There is a darker version of this mod available called S*****e Mouse that takes a decidedly darker turn than this. Players also get to meet Mokey’s gang including Grooby and Dilan. These are both parodied versions of Disney characters as well. Grooby is a humorous take on Goofy while Dilan is Donald Duck’s parodied form.

3. FNF: Pibby Corrupted Mod#

A glitch like corruption has come to take the Friday Night Funkin’ world. However, our characters are not about to go down easy. The Girlfriend has used her powers to put a shield around the Boyfriend for now but it all hinges on the BF winning the rap battle and saving the day. A lot of cartoon classics are afflicted by the glitch turning them into zombie versions of themselves. While Pibby and Boyfriend try their best to stop the corruption, it is not going to be an easy task with the cartoon zombies trying to stop them.  

4. FNF vs CJ & Ruby/ Starlight Mayhem Rebooted Mod#

The Boyfriend and Girlfriend go to a Mayhem concert where the BF is recognized by band member CJ. This leads to a friendly rap battle between the two of them. Later, CJ’s friend and band member Ruby also rap battles against the Boyfriend. Even the Girlfriend joins the battle to even the odds in this one! It is a fun mod that introduces two new opponents in the form of CJ & Ruby to the game. The bonus week does not have a story.

5. FNF vs Sonic.Exe 2.0 Mod#

This mos is a little different from the other Friday Night Funkin mods. It brings an element of horror to the game with the creepypasta Sonic the Hedgehog. It is a similar mod to the Sonic.exe but with a horror twist. In this mod, we see different versions of the Creepypasta Sonic the Hedgehog.

6. FNF: Huggy Playtime Mod#

This is a fun little mod where everyone takes turns to sing the tune of Playtime. There is not much of a story here just a fun way to unwind and take a break from the level mode of the game.

7. FNF vs Super Funky Idol Mod#

This mod pits the Boyfriend against a super funky idol guy from TikTok. It is one of the many parody mods in Friday Night Funkin’. It takes potshots at TikTok stars and the idolization they get from viewers.

#

8. FNF vs Impostor Among Us V3 Mod#

This mod adds four new weeks where Boyfriend rap battles against the Imposter from Among Us. Imposters are the villains from the popular game Among Us. They are tasked with killing the rest of the crew and sabotaging tasks. It is an interesting experience to see the astronauts partake in a rap battle through the help of this mod.

9. FNF Sarvente’s Mid-Fight Masses Mod#

Sarvente’s Mid-Fight Masses is a Friday Night Funkin’ mod that adds two weeks to the game. The Boyfriend and Girlfriend find themselves in a church as they search for a bathroom. However, they end up in a rap battle against Sarvente, the nun. The mod features 5 songs in total. Players that are just starting out with the game might face a hard time maneuvering the last two tracks in this mod.

#

10. FNF: Indie Cross vs Cuphead, Sans & Bendy Mod#

This mod pits the boyfriend against classic Indie characters. In this FNF mod the BF rap battles against popular characters like Cuphead. Cuphead is based on the video game character from the game Cuphead that came out in 2017. In addition, players also get to meet and battle against Sans from the 2015 game Undertale. This mod also features Ink Demon from the Bendy Franchise.

#

11. FNF vs Speedrunner Mario Mod#

Boyfriend and Girlfriend decide to visit the video game world on a vacation. However, things are not what they seem in the Mushroom Kingdom. Speedrunner Mario and Boyfriend rap battle it out in this 2-week addition mod to the game. Speedrunner Mario has his origins in the Something About Series where he starts off as the protagonist but slowly descends to become the main antagonist over time.

12. FNF: Minecraft Funky Edition#

Dive into the world of Minecraft with this funky mod. This mod allows players to battle against creepers, wolves, and even Herobrine. This new mod adds a lot to the game including 4 weeks of time. Players will also get a fully working Minecraft Menu and a fully playable Steve character. With custom mechanics and cutscenes, this mod is a must-have for Minecraft fans.  

13. FNF vs Freddy Fazbear#

This mod brings the world of Five Nights At Freddy’s to Friday Night Funkin’. In this mod Boyfriend and Girlfriend get locked inside Freddy Fazbear’s Pizzeria after dark. This obviously leads to a rap battle between the Boyfriend and Freddy Fazbear. Enjoy this mod as it brings two weeks of FNAF fun to FNF.

14. FNF Plants vs. Rappers Mod#

Enjoy a Plant vs, Zombies and FNF cross-over thanks to this mod. In this mod, music is dead in the world and it is up to the Boyfriend to revive it. Boyfriend rap battles against s few characters from the Plants vs. Zombies franchise to bring music back to the world. Boyfriend teams up with the zombie crew of Dr. Zomboss in this mod. This mod is currently in the demo phase.

#

15. Friday Night Funkin vs. Ben Drowned Mod#

This mod derives its inspiration from the famous creepypasta The Legend of Zelda: Majora’s Mask. Players get to meet a character Ben who belongs to that universe. In this mod, Boyfriend and Girlfriend were taking an innocent stroll in the forest when things go terribly wrong. Boyfriend has to rap battle with a horror mask icon to make it out of this mod.  

16. Friday Night Funkin Brawl Stars Mod#

This mod brings the popular characters from Brawl Stars to the world of Friday Night Funkin’. The Brawl Stars are looking for a more artistic outlet than fighting. They get their chance to do just that with the help of this mod which replaces characters from week 1 to 6 with Brawl Stars characters. This crossover mod will be a treat for players of both games.

These are 16 of the most popular mods in Friday Night Funkin’. However, these are not the only popular mods in the game.
