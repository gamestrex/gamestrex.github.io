How to Get Easter Bunny Day Eggs in Animal Crossing New Horizons (ACNH) 2022#

The Bunny Day event for 2022 is from the 10th to the 17th of April. If you want more time to collect all of the eggs then you can even time travel back to the event after it’s ended.Bunny Day Begins with Zipper T, the Bunny coming to your island. He will greet you and then start the Bunny event for the year. He will also give you the recipe for a Bunny Day bed to craft with the use of different eggs that you find around the island.

What Types of Eggs Are There in Animal Crossing New Horizons (ACNH)#

There are total 6 types of Eggs For Bunny Day in Animal Crossing New Horizons (ACNH) that you will find as follows:

Earth Eggs Stone Eggs Leaf Eggs Wood Eggs Sky Eggs Water Eggs

How to Find & Get Easter Bunny Day Eggs in Animal Crossing New Horizons (ACNH) 2022#

All the 6 eggs have their own separate methods to get them. So we will break down the steps for each of them as follows:

How to Get Earth Eggs in ACNH#

Earth Eggs are found in the ground. Just like with fossils, you will need to dig the ‘X’ in the ground to find them. Here is the step-by-step process of how you can get Earth Eggs:

Locate a cross or X pattern on the grass Get your shovel Dig to retrieve the ‘Earth Egg’ Eggs will not be unlimited Dig every day to collect as many eggs as possible

How to Get Stone Eggs in ACNH#

Do you know those stones that you get clay, stone and iron nuggets from? There’s your Stone Egg hiding place! Get Stone Eggs by following these steps:

Find a Stone on your island Get a shovel or axe Hit the stone to retrieve Eggs Eggs will not be unlimited Dig every day to collect as many eggs as possible

How to Get Leaf Eggs in ACNH#

Fruitless trees on your island will grow Leaf Eggs. Simply Find some around and you will get what you’re looking for. If you don’t have any because you decided to chop them down, simply plant a few fruitless trees and hope that they grow in time. Get Leaf Eggs by following these steps:

Find a fruitless tree Unequip your tool items Shake the tree with your hands Three Leaf Eggs should fall to the ground Eggs will not be unlimited Search every day to collect as many eggs as possible

How to Get Wood Eggs in ACNH#

Unlike Leaf Eggs, Wood Eggs can be found by hitting any of the trees on your island (Even palm trees!). However, Wood Eggs are one of the hardest to find as they don’t necessarily fall down. Here is how you can get Wood Eggs in the game:

Find a tree Get an axe Hit the tree Wood Eggs may fall to the ground Eggs will not be unlimited Search every day to collect as many eggs as possible

How to Get Sky Eggs in ACNH#

Sky Eggs for Bunny Day will be found in the floating presents in the air. These eggs aren’t going to come by often and if you want one then pay attention to the balloon carrying the present. The Sky Egg balloons are different from your regular balloons. The sky egg balloons are Egg-themed and easy to spot in the sky. Make sure to pay attention to the game music to know when a present is on its way to you. Here is how you too can get Sky Eggs:

Pay attention to the music in-game When the music goes quiet, a balloon should be up there If this balloon is rainbow/Easter egg colored Use your slingshot to bring it down Now collect your Sky Egg Eggs will not be unlimited Pay attention every day to collect as many eggs as possible

How to Get Water Eggs in ACNH#

Water Eggs can be caught by fishing for them in your island waterbodies. Spot a fish shadow and cast your fishing rod. It’ll most likely be a Water Egg. Usually, the eggs take the form of a fish that is not too big, nor too small. Look for medium-sized shadows and you should reel in the most Water eggs with that. Get Water Eggs by following these steps:

Head to your island’s ocean or river Watch for medium-sized fish shadows Cast your rod to reel in a fish Wait for it to bite You should get Water Eggs when you reel it in Water Eggs are unlimited You can reel in as many Water Eggs as you want for the day
