Animal Crossing New Hairstyles List Top Fab Pop Cool And Stylish Hair Colours In New Horizons

November 11, 2022 · 3 min · 595 words · Graciela Jones

After the tease of being able to change your appearance at the start of the game, the opportunity to change your hair comes sooner than you think. Alongside the three style sets available in the early-game - Top 8 Pop Hairstyles, Top 8 Cool Hairstyles and Top 8 Stylish Hair Colors - the 2.0 update also brought Top 4 Fab Hairstyles and Harv’s Island hairstyles, and you can learn how to unlock all of these in this page. Newly added in update 2.0: Elsewhere on this page: The earliest you can get a Mirror is by scouting the beaches around the island. Before long (we found ours on the first day) you’ll find a Message in a Bottle. Inside it is a DIY Recipe for a Mirror. Craft it at a DIY Workshop and place it somewhere on your island. Now press A in front of it, giving you the option to change your appearance. Already, you have more hairstyles than what you start the game with - an additional six on top of the initial character designer. To get more hairstyles, you need to spend Nook Miles. Head to the shop, and investigate the Nook Stop ATM in the corner, then Redeem Nook Miles. More than four in the bullet point list below:

Top 4 Fab Hairstyles - 1200 Nook Miles Part of the 2.0 update released on Wednesday, 3rd November. Only available if you’ve redeemed all previous hairstyles from the Nook Stop Harv’s Island Hairstyles - they’re free! As well all as the above Nook’s Shop Hairstyles, there are additions as part of Harriet in the 2.0 update - more on that at the end of this section Top 8 Pop Hairstyles - 2,400 Nook Miles Top 8 Cool Hairstyles - 2,400 Nook Miles Top 6 Stylish Hairstyles - 1,800 Nook Miles Part of the free winter update released on Thursday, 19th November Top 8 Stylish Hair Colours - 3,000 Nook Miles

Select the one you want - the rest of this page features the designs in full so you know what you’re buying - and it’ll be added to the character customisation screen the next time you look at a Mirror. Bear in mind that while the Top 4 Fab Hairstyles are easily accessible from the shop in exchange for Nook Miles, the Harv’s Island Hairstyles are more complicated to secure. You will have to wait a few days to get new options from Harriet, so if she doesn’t offer you more, try again the next day. At the same time, the hairstyle she grants you is always random.
