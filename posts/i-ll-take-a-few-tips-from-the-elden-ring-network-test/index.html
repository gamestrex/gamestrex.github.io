I think I should use the Guard Counter early.#

The Guard Counter is an new and new skill in the Elden Ring that trivializes famines with basic enemies as long as you learn it. It’s much easier and less risky than parry, but it’s also very powerful, but still lets you torn out the enemies. The Guard Anti-Ware is really easy to pull off: simply hold up your shield to block an attack and push R2 to release a powerful counter blow. Blam! The key to using the guard counter safely is to wait until the enemy completes an attack sequence. The Guard Counter will not interrupt their attacks, therefore wait for the third hit before you counter if they come with a three hit combo. Otherwise you may still receive heavy hits. However, you aren’t going to take damage. Wait till your opening, and you will try to block almost every incoming damage and then destroy enemies safely.
