Back 4 Blood: Quickly Farm Supply Points:#

Method 1 After You’ve unlocked Most Cards, you should have unlocked all of them.#

Here’s the step-by-step guide to the most well-known Back 4 Blood Supply Points farming trick. So do you just watch the five-minute video? To watch the 5-minute video or just wish to read the guide and see what you’ve got to do?

Create a campaign run. On Veteran, take Act 2 and Job 10:22 and Work of the Worm (Part 1). It’s better to play on Veteran because the harder one gets. So, Veteran is more popular than Nightmare. One of the tools that you can unlock the micgun after you open the gate of the mine is the toolkit. Karlee always starts by a toolkit, so it’s time for one to pick him up. Use Miniguns to clean up the remainder of the Ridden and Mutations if it is required. It should just be 2 minutes before you get 52 Supply Points. Delete Run and repeat as necessary.

We need to save the entire grenade damage output.

Demolitioners expert: 50 % damage to the ground, -12 % damage to the ground. Improvised Explosives: Seven5% Damage from Accessory, tenths p.m. Bomb Squad: total explosive damage, 34.5% explosive resistance. Grainade training: 25% damages for accessibility. Double Grenade: 2 additional Offensive Inventory, -10% Damage Dealt. Onpities: -10% Health, + Team Offensive Inventory, +1 Team Offensive Inventory. A glass cage: 25 p.m. ; 30 p.m. Take a quick, easy action and make it even more speedy. Olympic Skirmometer: 30 % Sprint Efficiency, 5 % damage Resistance. Cardio in order to save weight: 20 ml., 20 ml. A fleet of feet: 0% moves speed, -5% damage resistance. Cross Trainers: 20% Stamina, 20% Stamina Regen, 3% Move Speed. Green beer: 40% Stamina, 5-5% resistance. Daise: 5 p.m.

Of course, for some of the better cards such as Bomb Squad, Glass Cannon or Olympic Sprinter (especially Bomb Squad, its not advisable to stagger the Ogre) you need to set up the later tiers of Supply Lines first. We’re getting supply points, so we can turn them in. Don’t worry, that’s a catch-22, since it brings us in the second-best way to acquire Supply Points for Back 4 Bloods early game.

Method 2, For newers, get the opportunity to try the technique.#

The step by step guide, back 4 Blood Seconds Supply Points farming method, is good for that. If you don’t like to watch the video for 4 minutes, you should read manuals, or just want a quick list of what you need to do: or are you planning to get some good results?

Create a campaign run. On Recruit, pick Act 1 > Search and Rescue > Barroom Blitz level. You can play it on Veteran, but if you don’t build any proper decks, it’s going to be difficult. And this second method will work, so far as to eliminate the virtually endless amount of cards that are used to save it. Take the Jukebox, then hide it behind the bar. We suggest you do a melee training. Check them out on our deck building guide. Kill those zombies till the level begins. You only need a few minutes to make 44 supply points. Relatively, please repeat.

And this is what you got. I hope it helps. Back 4 Blood is now available on PlayStation 4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox One, X-series and PC via Steam, and Xbox Game Pass.
