Borderlands 3 Typhon Log Locations And Typhon Dead Drop Locations Explained

Inside these Dead Drops you’ll find powerful weapons, shields and maybe even a new item for your room on the good ship Sanctuary. You will also receive a nice amount of XP, some cash and a little bit of Eridium. You can find the first Typhon Log after you’ve jumped the broken bridge on your way to the Sun Smasher clan camp. After crossing the bridge, head immediately to your left. Follow this path and take the first opportunity to go left up the hill. Once you’re up the hill, go right to the far edge of the hillside. There you’ll find the Typhon Log overlooking Varkid Valley. The second Typhon Log can be found in the remains of the Dahl Orbital Control. You’ll visit this area during Taking Flight. The log can be found past the small settlement of bandits on the outcrop of rock overlooking the Pandora waste. You’ll pass it on your way out of the Orbital Control. The third and final Typhon Log in the The Droughts is just east of Tannis’s digsite. Rather than heading straight into her dig site, go to the skag dens that is just to the east of the entrance. There, on top of the rocky outcrop in the middle of the collection of dens, you’ll find the final Typhon Log. To find the Dead Drop head north-west from where you start the Under Taker mission. Go past the pillars you see in front a valley entrance and head to the Dahl building at the very back of this area. Keep an eye on the Varkid in this area - there might even be a badass on the prowl. When you reach the Dahl building, go around its side to the area with all the television monitors to find the Dead Drop hidden against the back wall. Open the Dead Drop to receive your loot, along with:

202 XP $31 5 Eridium

To find the first Typhon Log, head towards the Holy Broadcast Centre. Just before you reach the nearest Catch-A-Ride station to the Holy Broadcast Centre, however, you’ll come to a junction in the road - take the left road. Follow this new path along until you reach a small pathway taking you up a hill to an outcrop. Go up this path and you’ll find the Typhon Log on this outcrop. The second Typhon Log can be along the road leading to the Holy Broadcast Centre. The log is on a small metal platform, overlooking the centre, just before a set of signposts advertising the COV. You’ll need to be pretty close to the edge of the road to make the Log Icon appear on your map. The third and final Typhon Log is found directly south of the entrance to Ascension Bluff from The Draughts. Use whatever method you prefer to head directly south until you can’t go any further, then go east slightly to find a small, rounded area overlooking the desert. There you’ll find the last Typhon Log. This Dead Drop is found just behind the Catch-A-Ride station that is before the barrier leading up to the Holy Broadcast Centre. Right next to the platform where you construct your vehicles, you’ll find a collection of containers. Climb on top of the yellow one and you’ll discover that it has a hole in its ceiling. Drop down inside and you’ll find the Typhon Dead Drop. Open it for a collection of loot, along with:

391 XP $39 5 Eridium

It’s beneath a pillar in the middle of the small grassland beneath the broken bridges of Meridian. You’ll know you’re in the right place when you find an elevator. Take the elevator to the next floor and then go up the first set of stairs on your right to reach a small landing. There you’ll see a small roof overhang; carefully use this overhang to drop own to the balcony below. On this balcony you’ll find the second Typhon Log. The third Typhon Log can be found just south of the most northerly entrance to the Meridian Metroplex. It’s beneath the bridge, overlooking the dried up river that surrounds Meridian. Head to the area where the Promethea citizens are hiding in the Meridian Outskirts. Go to the area just before you venture outside again to access the Meridian Metroplex. If you look to your left in this area, you’ll find an elevator. Now that you’ve found the elevator, making sure you’re not standing inside it, press the button to send it to the lower floor. Once the elevator roof has passed you, jump down so that you’re standing on this roof. When the elevator reaches the bottom floor, you’ll find a button. Press this button and it will send the elevator back upwards. You’ll find yourself in the space above the elevator and the home of the Typhon Dead Drop.
