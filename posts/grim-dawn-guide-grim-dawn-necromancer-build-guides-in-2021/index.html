All Grim Dawn Necromancer Build Guide#

1. Pet Cabalist Variant 1 (Necromancer + Occultist)#

This pet build focuses mainly on the full power of necromancer’s pets, along with buffing it with Blood of Dreeg. Attributes Make sure that you are putting most of your points on your pet’s physique since they will be saving you from all enemy damage. Hence 90% to 95% attributes of yours should be placed in it. Put points in cunning and sprits only if you are planning to equip something that requires cunning or want to high-level enchanted items respectively Necromancer Skills

Mastery Bar (50 points) Raise Skeletons (16 points) Undead Legion (12 points) Will of the Crypt (12 points) Summon Blight Fiend (16 points) Rotting Fumes (12 points) Blight Burst (12 points) Reap Spirit (16 points) Master of Death (12 points) Call of the Grave (10 points) Mark of Torment (3 points) Bone Harvest (1 point) Soul Harvest (12 points)

Occultist Skills

Mastery Bar (32 points) Blood of Dreeg (16 points) Aspect of Guardian (12 points)

Devotion

Jackal Eel Panther Lion Raven Shepherd’s Crook Empty Throne Bysmiel’s Bonds Staff of Rattosh Typhos, the Jailor of Souls Behemoth Rattosh, the Veilwarden

Equipment Priorities

Use equipment that grant bonuses to your pets

How to Play? The game can be played by following the general rule to build a pet. Make sure to use Blood Of Dreeg to buff and heal your pets.

3. Dark Mage Cabalist (Necromancer + Occultist)#

This is a non-pet cabalist build that deals huge damage on enemies with some chaos damage. It also has great survival points compared to above two Grim Dawn Necromancer Build. Attributes

Spend 60% or 75% of your points on spirit Spend Remaining points on your  physique. Spending points on cunning is optional.

Necromancer Skills

Mastery Bar (50 points) Bone Harvest (16 points) Dread (12 points) Soul Harvest (12 points) Siphon Souls (12 points) Blood Boil (12 points) Harbinger of Souls (12 points) Reap Spirit (16 points) Reaping Strike (10 points) Mark of Torment (1 point)

Occultist Skills

Mastery Bar (40 points) Solael’s Witchfire (12 points) Consecrated Blade (3 points) Second Rite (12 points) Sigil of Consumption (12 points) Doom Bolt (12 points)

Devotion

Gallows Sailor’s Guide Eel Jackal Bat Empty Throne Toad Wendigo Affliction Typhos, Jailor of Souls Dying God

Equipment Priorities Its a better choice to get gear with bonuses affixed to Vitality damage and Chaos damage. How to Play? Follow three simple steps to play this non-pet necromancer build.

Make use of Sigil Consumption and Doom Bolt on long range enemies. Use Siphon of Souls if you get surrounded by melee. Making use of bone harvest helps in dealing damage to multiple enemies that are in straight sight.

4. Grim Reaper (Necromancer + Nightblade)#

This is one of the best build for players who wish to play like a Grim Reaper. It is melee type build and has the ability to deal more damage to the enemy while converting a small amount of it to your health. Attributes

Prioritize on high amounts of physique Spirit is your next priority For better offensive ability getting cunning will be a wise choice.

Necromancer Skills

Mastery Bar (50 points) Bone Harvest (16 points) Harvester of Death (1 point) Dread (12 points) Soul Harvest (12 points) Siphon Souls (12 points) Blood Boil (10 points) Reap Spirit (16 points) Harbringer of Souls (12 points) Spectral Binding (12 points) Spectral Wrath (10 points) Reaping Strike (10 points) Necrotic Edge (10 points) Mark of Torment (2 points)

Nightblade Skills

Mastery Bar (20 points) Shadow Strike (1 point) Pneumatic Burst (12 points) Blade Barrier (10 points) Anatomy of Murder (12 points)

Devotion

Lion Gallows Bat Toad Wendigo Behemoth Typhos, the Jailor of Souls Kraken Affliction Rattosh, the Veilwarden

Equipment Priorities

Primary choice for you will be  vitality, vitality decay, and aether Secondary choices would be cold and physical strength

How to Play?

Keep attacking your enemy to gain more health

5. Range Blade Reaper (Necromancer + Nightblade)#

Range Blade Reaper help in damage balances physical and magical. which is later converted into health. Attributes

Spend 25 or 30 points on cunning, Spend 25 or 30 points on spirit Spend remaining points on physique.

Necromancer Skills

Mastery Bar (50 points) Bone Harvest (16 points) Dread (12 points) Soul Harvest (12 points) Harbinger of Souls (12 points)

Nightblade Skills

Mastery Bar (40 points) Phantasmal Blade (16 points) Frenetic Throw (1 point) Heart Seeker (12 points) Blade Trap (12 points) Devouring Blades (12 points) Pneumatic Burst (12 points) Breath of Belgothian (1 point) Shadow Dance (12 points) Blade Barrier (12 points) Anatomy of Murder (12 points)

Devotion

Assassin’s Blade Gallows Bat Empty Throne Toad Crane Blades of Nadaan Wendigo Affliction Unknown Soldier

Equipment Priorities

Make sure to equip gear that increases damage to vitality, pierce, or bleeding. Also equip a caster’s off-hand for energy regeneration and cooldown reduction.

How to Play?

Use Phantasmal Blades like its your normal attack. Cast your blade trap whenever available and most importantly when you have to hold of melee enemies.
