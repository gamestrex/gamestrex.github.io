Destiny 2 Wayfinder S Voyage Quest Steps How To Complete The Weekly Seasonal Quest

December 5, 2022 · 9 min · 1894 words · Sadie Dreyer

Similar to Path of the Splicer during Season of the Splicer, this introduces a long-term plan to the season’s new activities and features, with each week tasking you with using the Wayfinder’s Compass, collecting Parallax Trajectory, and more. From the first step onwards, this quest grants access to the Astral Alignment seasonal activity, as well as an entry to the Shattered Realm. This page explains how Wayfinder’s Voyage works as well as every Wayfinder’s Voyage quest step as they’re released on a weekly basis throughout the season. On this page: This isn’t different to how it worked for Path of the Splicer. At least at a glance, that is, as the structure of the quest steps is different. Within each week’s Wayfinder’s Voyage is broadly the same structure - visiting the H.E.L.M., completing an Astral Alignment, collecting Parallax Trajectory then entering the Shattered Realm, then returning to the H.E.L.M. for some character interactions to progress the story. Note that the destinations of the quest steps will change reflecting on the current rotation of both the planetary Ascendant Anchors and / or the Shattered Realm mission that’s currently active. Wayfinder’s Voyage has a total of 53 steps, divided in bulk on a weekly basis. It’s worth keeping this in mind as early as possible to avoid any confusions and make the most out of your time in Season of the Lost! Wayfinder’s Voyage I quest steps are as follows:

Speak to Petra Venj through the Wayfinder’s Compass in the H.E.L.M. Use the Wayfinder’s Compass to realign the Blind Well with the Awoken Ley Line network (complete Offensive: Astral Alignment) Return and speak to Petra Venj through the Wayfinder’s Compass in the H.E.L.M. once more

Once done, you will be rewarded with the Iota Draconis fusion rifle. The Wayfinder quest will now begin. Wayfinder’s Voyage week 1 quest steps are as follows:

Step 1: Travel to Trostland in the EDZ and collect Parallax Trajectory Step 2: Enter the Shattered Realm via the H.E.L.M. map to find and rescue a lost Techeun’s Ley Line (complete the currently active Shattered Realm mission) Step 3: Wayfinder’s Compass upgraded with Barrier Breach Step 4: Speak to Savathun in Mara’s Chambers Step 5: Speak with Ikora Rey in the Tower Bazaar Step 6: Each Techeun found, each clearing of the Ley Lines brings Mara Sov’s plan closer to fruition. Continue collecting Parallax Trajectory, conducting Astral Alignments, and charting the Ley Lines of the Shattered Realm. Eventually, Savathun’s worm will be exorcised and the real Osiris will be returned (to be continued in step 7 of Wayfinder’s Voyage and beyond)

While you’re at it, make sure to pick up the Trial of the Stars I quest, where you’ll have to find Atlas Skews in the Dreaming City. Once done, you will be rewarded with 150 Parallax Trajectory. Wayfinder’s Voyage week 2 quest steps are as follows:

Provided you’ve started Wayfinder’s Voyage while the quest was initially active, these steps - as well as all associated activities, including Astral Alignments and Shattered Realm missions - will remain in the game until the end of Season 15, or until the start of ‘Year 5’ when The Witch Queen expansion arrives. This also applied to Season of the Splicer, which is why you’re still seeing Mithrax’s room of operations in the H.E.L.M. This is all part of Bungie’s attempts to address FOMO (fear of missing out), allowing you to continue to play previous activities for longer, even if later seasons shift their focus to new ones.
