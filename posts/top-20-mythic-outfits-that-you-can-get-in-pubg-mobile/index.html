What are Mythic Outfits in PUBG Mobile?#

For the unversed, there is a title in PUBG Mobile called, Mythic Fashion. It asks players to gather a lot of Mythics outfits in their in-game inventory. There are plenty of Mythics outfits available in PUBG Mobile, and collecting all of them is a daunting task. Once you have successfully obtained 50 Mythics Outfits in PUBG Mobile, you will be a given a Mythic title. The best thing about these outfits is it comes with a permanent Emote that can also be used separately. The reason why people love PUBG Mobile the most is it keeps adding new Mythic Outfits to the game. Recently, they dropped a new crate, which has an incredible Mythics Outfit.
