Maplestory Best Link Skills Guide – 2022#

Links skills are acquired when a character hits a certain level. Most of them have a maximum level of 2 or 3 and can grant you a ton of bonus stats.

Character level 70 – Level 1 Link Skill Character level 120 – Level 2 Link Skill Character level 210 – Level 3 Link Skill

These Link Skills have certain limitations which need to be kept in mind. Before we get started with the list of all link skills and their priority first let’s check out what these said limitations are. LINK SKILL LIMITATIONS

They can only be linked to 1 character at a time. 12 skills can be linked up to a character at a time. You will be able to stack Cygnus’ Blessing up to 5 times on a character for a total of 25% Status Resistance. Characters that have made their first job advancement will be the ones you can use link skills on. You can unlock Link Skill at level one but will only be able to share your link skill upon reaching Level 70. If you accept the Link Skill quest with a Quest Deliverer it will break Tot’s (applicable only for GMS) You won’t be able to link skill to 2 or more characters in the same world. You can’t have 2 or more of the same jobs linking 1 character. You can change the linked character once a day. A character can be linked by one or more classes that have a link skill.

List Of MapleStory Link Skills By Jobs#

Mercedes – Elven Blessing#

Originated from Mercedes. Boosts EXP gain and gives the ability to teleport to Elluel.  Best skill to gain more experience when training your primary character to Level 200. Also best when you wanna use it to travel back to Victoria Island.

Demon Slayer – Fury Unleashed#

Skill from Demon Slayer. Increases your inner rage to deal even greater damage to bosses.

Phantom – Phantom Instinct#

Permanently increases Critical Rate – Use high levels of insight to locate enemies’ weaknesses for this.

Luminous – Light Wash/Permeate#

From Luminous. Ignores a portion of enemy defense. (magic and weapon defense)

Angelic Buster – Terms and Conditions#

Temporarily boost damage for 10 seconds. Has a cooldown time of 90 seconds.

Demon Avenger – Wild Rage#

Permanently increases damage. Your rage goes wild which increases the damage.

Xenon – Hybrid Logic#

Activates your Hybrid Logic routines to permanently increase all stats.

Hayato – Keen Edge#

Hayato’s blade can cut through anything. Increase overall power.

Kanna – Elementalism#

Deepens your connection to the elements to provide a permanent damage increase.

Beast Tamer – Focus Spirit#

Focus and increase overall power.

 Eunwol / Shade – Close Call#

Save yourself from an attack that would’ve been fatal.

Kinesis – Judgement#

Deals the strongest possible fatal blow using righteous judgment.

Aran – Combo Kill Advantage#

Provides additional EXP when absorbing a Combo Kill Orb. Maha blesses your combos.

Evan – Rune Persistence#

Increases the duration of all liberated Runes by intensifying their mana.

Cadena – Unfair Advantage, Intensive Insult#

Attacking weaker enemies becomes more effective.

Illium – Flow of Battle, Tide Of Battle#

Maintains the flow of the battle. Moving a certain distance within a set time frame increases damage.

Ark – Ecstasy, Solus#

Temporarily increase your damage after a certain period of time during combat.

Hoyoung – Self Confidence#

Increases DEF ignored and increases damage to monsters with full HP.

Adele – Noblesse#

Increase damage against boss monsters, and depending on your party size, further increases damage. And with this, we will be ending our article on Maplestory Best Link Skills Guide.
