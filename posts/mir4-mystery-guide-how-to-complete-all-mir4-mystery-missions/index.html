MIR4 Mystery Quest Guide – Sept 2021#

Before we explain how to complete the Mysteries quest in MIR4, let’s take a look at all Mysteries available in the game. As of now, there are a total of five Mysteries that every MIR4 players have to complete.

Nefariox Horn Myriad Needle A Noble Cause The Lost Tome Incomparable Master

Without any further ado, let’s take a look at each Mystery quest location and how to complete them in MIR4.

Nefariox Horn Mystery#

There are multiple missions that you will have to complete to finish this Mystery in the game. To take missions, you will have to find ‘+’ icon, which is located on the top right corner of the screen and click on it. When you do so, a new window will appear on the screen.

You will to click on ‘Missions’ icon first and then ‘Requests’. Doing this will show you a list of missions that you will need to complete throughout the game.

The First Sage#

To get The First Sage started, you will have to approach and have a talk with the Dwarf Digger, who can be found in the Cave of the World Beetles.

Bloodshade Nefariox Horn#

Now, you will have to go the Missions>Requests>Ginkgo Valley and then select Madman’s Trace mission. To complete this, you will have to find the Grotesque Statue.

Beautiful Sister Magya#

The next thing that you will need to do is to complete ‘Treasure’s Aura’ mission, which can also be found in the Ginkgo Valley section.

Nefariox Pearl#

The last thing that you will need to complete the Nefariox Horn Mysteries quest in MIR4 is to complete the quest titled ‘For the Greater Good’. Unlike the previous ones, this mission is located in Bicheon Town section as shown in the above picture.

Myriad Needle Mystery#

Similar Nefariox Horn, you will have to complete a bunch of missions to complete Myriad Needle Mystery in MIR4. If you don’t know what that missions are then keep reading this post.

Herbalist Hong’s True Identity#

To start Myriad Needle Mystery in MIR3, players will have to complete a mission titled ‘The Wanted Of Bicheon Castle’. To complete this Mystery, you will have to find Dye for the Wanted Poster.

An Herb More Valuable than Gold#

The begin the second Mystery from Myriad Needle, you are required to complete a mission called ‘ Unexpected Income’. To complete this Mystery, you will have to collect Treasure chests.

Those Tainted by Demonic Energy#

Unlike the previous ones, players will have to go to Crystaline Forest tab and select Demonic Cult’s Power and Demons quest. Once selected, you will have to collect Blood for Research to complete this mystery.

Once completed, you will have to select yet another mystery from the same section. The mystery that you will to select is titled ‘Rumors about the Demonic Cult Leader’. To complete this mystery, you will have to find the Flower Tinged with Demonic.

Overambitious Father#

The next mystery that you will have to complete is called ‘Overambitious Father’. This requires you to complete a mission titled ‘Clandestine Investigation 1’. To complete this mission, you will have to gather Iron Squad’s Mark.

Flower of the Demonic Cult#

To start Flower of the Demonic Cult Mystery in Mir4, you will need to take a mission called ‘Suspicious Women’, which is located in the Snake Pit section. Once taken, you will have to find the Previous herb to complete this mystery.

Mad Healer Pung’s Trace#

To begin Made Healer Pung’s Trace mystery, you will have to complete a mission called ‘A Flower Tinged With Moonlight’. This mystery tasks you to find the Moonlight Flower. Once you are done completing this quest, you will have to go through the following quests:

Bull Fiend Research 1 Bull Fiend Research 2 Bull Fiend Research 3 Bull Fiend Research 4

A Noble Cause#

Like you completed missions and quests in previous mysteries, you will need to finish a bunch of missions to complete A Noble Cause Mystery in MIR4.  If you don’t know what that missions are then keep reading this post.

Jo Gyu’s Corruption#

To begin this mystery, you will have to complete a mission called ‘Military officer’s Tyranny’. This mission is located in Snake Pit>Request section.

Noh Wunjang’s Evil Path#

To complete this mystery, you will have to complete Jo Gyu’s Corruption.

Cleaning the Backstreet#

To begin Cleaning the Backstreet mystery, you will have to go through the previous mystery Noh Wunjang’s Evil Path.

Lies and Hypocrisy#

Each and every mystery in A Noble Cause is connected to the previous one. To complete this one, you need to go through Cleaning the Backstreet.

A Noble Cause#

To begin A Noble Cause, complete the Mystery called Lies and Hypocrisy.

A Proposal Gem#

The mystery that you are required to complete is ‘A Proposal Gem’. To start this one, you will have to complete the Splendor of the Mount Tai Sect, which can be found in the Request Bicheon Town section. That’s all you need to know about how to complete Mysteries — Nefariox Horn, Myriad Needle, A Noble Cause in MIR4.
