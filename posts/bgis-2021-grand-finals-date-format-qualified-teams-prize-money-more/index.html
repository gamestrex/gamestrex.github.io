Yesterday (January 10th, 2022) was the fourth and last day of the BGMI 2021 Semifinals, which had 24 teams fighting for 16 spots in the finals. The name of the team that topped the BGIS points table in the Semifinals is none other than TeamOX. Since the Semifinals of BGIS have concluded, 16 teams have been qualified for the Grand Finals of BGIS 2021. To know the names of qualified teams, finals format, prize money, and more, you will have to read this post till the end. Without any further ado, let’s get started:

BGIS 2021: Grand Finals Date, Format, Qualified Teams, Prize Money & More#

Date & Format#

If you have been following BGIS 2021 tournament since its beginning then you must be quite excited about its Finals. The grand finals of BGIS 2021 will be held between 13th to 16th January 2022. As mentioned above, all 16 teams that have been qualified for the finals will be competing in 24-matches during the mentioned time. The team that manages to score the highest number of points will be declared as the winner or Champion of the BGIS 2021 tournament.

Has Team SouL Been Eliminated From BGIS 2021?#

Yes, Fan-Favorite squad Team Soul has been delimited from the tournament due to their poor performances. During the four-day Semifinals, Team Soul finished in the 22nd spot and could only obtain 64 points in 16-matches.

Allocations#

Irrespective of which team will be crowned BGIS 2021 Champion, they will be qualified as the Indin representative for the PUBG Mobile Global Championship (PMGC) 2021 Grand Finals. For the unversed, PGMC 2021 Grand Finals will be taken place on January 21st, 2022.

Grand Finals 2021 Prize Pool#

The tournament has a whopping prize pool of Rs 1 Crore. Besides getting qualified for PGMC 2021 Grand Finals, the winners of BGIS 2021 will be given a prize of Rs 50 Lakh. The MVP will get an additional prize of Rs 1 Lakh and there will be four other individual prizes of Rs 50,000 each.

When & Where to Watch BGIS 2021 Finals?#

The grand finals of BGIS 2021 will be streamed exclusively on the official YouTube channel of BGMI. Notably, the live streaming will be done in English and Hindi languages.
