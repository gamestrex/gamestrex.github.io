Best MMORPG Games To Play 2022#

1. Runescape#

Runescape might have been around for a while but it has not lost its touch. The game, which was first released in 2001, is still popular among gamers owing to its regular updates. While the gameplay might be a bit simplistic for the advanced gamer but the huge amount of content more than makes up for it. Players can not only fight it out in combat scenarios but also fish and farm in a rustic, nostalgic virtual life. Moreover, the game offers engaging mini-games and quests that rope the players in. Before you know it, it’s been hours since you started playing! And isn’t that what a great MMORPG game is all about?

2. RIFT#

RIFT is a bit of a mixed bag. The game has a picturesque setup, an engaging storyline, and a whole lot to do in the fantasy world of Telara. It offers extensive class customization and provides players with an exciting gameplay experience. The game also offers a massive amount of content. However, the problem is that the game has a lot less traffic than it used to. At the moment, the game is staying afloat due to a loyal fanbase. The lack of players can make it a challenge to band together for adventures though. Battle Pass Season 4: Vein of Glory was the last big update for the game back in 2020. We hope things pick up soon for this MMORPG as it would be a shame to waste the potential of the game.

3. World Of Warcraft#

World Of Warcraft still has the interest of MMORPG gamers even after 10+ years of its release. The game has everything a player’s heart could desire. You can farm, play match three mini-games, and immerse yourself in the lore as you partake in this immense MMORPG game. Constant upkeep of content has ensured that new gamers keep arriving into the massive community of WoW. Over time, the game has seen new classes, races, continents arrive to keep players hooked. The graphics overhauls have ensured that the game doesn’t get too dated for the current stream of gamers. WoW may be old but it is far from a nostalgia act. Give this game a go in 2022 if you still haven’t and be a part of one of the most enduring gaming communities around. This game was developed by Blizzard Entertainment and came out in 2004. 4. Star Wars: The Old Republic#

Star Wars: The Old Republic may take place in a galaxy far, far away but it has still found a place in the hearts of the gaming community. Released in 2011, this game allows players a lot of choices when it comes to gameplay. It is up to you whether you want to play as the Empire or Republic. Moreover, you can choose from the eight classes to play and step into the shoes of an Imperial Agent, a Jedi Knight, or a Sith Inquisitor. The game is one of the best Star War games released. 5. Guild Wars 2#

In a bucketful of traditional MMORPG games, Guild Wars 2 marches to the beat of its own drum. It is a game that is not trying to be any other beloved game but crafting its own niche within the gaming community. The game is also entirely free to play making it accessible to a wider range of audiences compared to subscription-based games. Player actions also have consequences in this game making it a complete departure from most games in the MMORPG category. Developed by ArenaNet, the game came out in 2012.

6. Final Fantasy XIV#

FFXIV has had quite a journey. When it was first released, the game was riddled with bugs and glitches that made it impossible to play. However, refusing to let Final Fantasy XIV die a slow death, Square Enix decided to turn things around. The game was first released in 2010 but after receiving overwhelmingly negative reactions the developers reworked the game and re-released it in 2013. It remade the game and gave the gaming community a beautiful gem in the form of Final Fantasy XIV: A Realm Reborn. The game has everything players love about the Final Fantasy franchise: immersive storylines, beautiful landscapes, and engaging characters. Moreover, expansion packs like Shadowbringers and Endwalker have kept the game fresh for players. 7. Lord Of The Rings Online#

Lord of the Rings is the perfect setup for an MMORPG game. It takes place in middle earth, has a ton of pre-established lore, and has multiple races to play as. It is a dream setting for any type of game. You can dive into the world created by J.R.R. Tolkein as an elf, dwarf, hobbit, or human to explore the massive world. Enjoy walking through the world you’ve experienced through books and movies to become a part of the adventure. Through your travels, you will get to meet the Ents and explore the mines of Moria if you are brave enough. In addition, this game is completely free to play making it a treasured addition to the list. It has a massive fan community for a game that came out 10+ years ago. LOTR Online had been developed by Standing Stone Games and Turbine.

8. Neverwinter#

Neverwinter is a love letter to Dungeons & Dragons as it takes up all the places, classes, spells, and abilities from the game to form an amazing MMORPG game. While the game is free to play, it is quite difficult to progress through the game without spending money. However, the game does bring in a lot of free expansions and introduce cool new lands like the Icewind Dale. The game was released in 2013 and is developed by Cryptic Games. This game is a dream come true for the D&D fans who’ve wanted to experience the adventures in the game in a more immersive setting.

9. DC Universe Online#

This one is a no-brainer for comic book fans everywhere. Dive into the world of DC with all the iconic characters like Superman, Wonder Woman, and Batman. Help them take on villains like The Joker and Riddler as you progress in this action-packed MMORPG. Best of all, the game has been made free to play with an optional subscription.

10. Black Desert Online#

Black Desert Online is a Korean MMO packed full of action combat. In an interesting twist to the typical MMORPG games though Black Desert Online also offers players a chance to become property managers. Players can buy buildings and even take over cities in the game eventually. While the game does have its set of kinks to work out, it remains one of the best MMORPG games that you should definitely play in 2022. Players can enjoy Black Desert Online on PC, Xbox One, and PS4.
