Marvel Future Revolution Tier List – Best Starting Hero#

All the 8 playable characters in Marvel Future revolution are divided into three tiers S, A, and B with S being the strongest and B being the weakest. Here’s the tier wise ranking of all the heroes in Marvel Future Revolution

S Tier Heroes In Marvel Future Revolution#

Iron Man#

For obvious reasons, Iron Man is if not the best one of the best heroes, you can get in Future Revolution. Apart from being extremely overpowered Iron man also has a great defense and allows you to go for longer in any game mode. Iron man is a deadly combination of extreme damage and defense and you should try the Hero.

Captain America#

Just like Iron Man Captain America also yields very high damage. Apart from high DPS Captain is also the best combat character you can get in Future revolution. Captain America can focus his attacks at specific weak points of the enemies allowing you to deal more damage quickly.

Captain Marvel#

Just like the MCU Captain Marvel is an OP hero in Future Revolution also. Captain Marvel seems to have the best of both Captain America and Iron Man in Future revolution. The USP of Captain Marvel is the damage she deals with every attack. The extremely high single-hit damage of Captain Marvel makes her a must-try hero in Marvel Future Revolution.

A Tier Heroes In Marvel Future Revolution#

Doctor Strange#

Doctor Strange is a classic ranged hero that deals good damage while maintaining distance from the enemy. With a good DPS doctor, strange is a very good hero especially if you prefer ranged combat.

Spider-Man#

Just like Doctor strange Spiderman also follows the ranged combat format but deals a bit more damage than the Wizard. Without a doubt, Spiderman is the best Ranged hero you can get in Marvel Future Revolution and if you dealing damage from a distance is your style then go for Spiderman.

B Tier Heroes In Marvel Future Revolution#

Black Widow#

Black widow is a classic combat hero and the same is the case in Marvel Future Revolution as well. Even though the damage is not as high as other heroes the fast combat allows you to dish out combo attacks.

Strom#

Storm’s unique powers make her stand out of all the 8 heroes. Apart from her powers Storm is an excellent choice for one-to-one combat as her powers give her an edge during mere combat.

Star-Lord#

While Star-Lord might not be awesome at combat his gadgets make up for the damage. The unique set of gadgets gives you the element of surprise, but this also means handling Star-Lord will be a task (Yondu would relate). Star-Lord is a must-have if you want to surprise the enemies.
