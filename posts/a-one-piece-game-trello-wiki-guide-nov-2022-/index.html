What Is A One Piece Game Trello & Wiki?#

A One Piece Game Trello & Wiki can provide players with some valuable information about the game that can be pivotal in their game progression. This includes Fruits, Currency, Bosses, and more of such vital topics. Let us take a brief look at each of these topics today.

Beli#

Beli is the in-game currency in A One Piece Game. It is quite useful as players can purchase swords, guns, fruits, fighting style, and other in-game items with it. Players can find Beli by opening chest, defeating mobs, and from quests. Players can get up to $3K Beli from Chests.

Game Passes#

Devil Fruit Notifier#

This Game Pass notifies players about when and where a devil fruit will spawn.

Coffin Boat#

This Game Pass allocates a boat with a top speed of 125.

Striker#

This Game Pass allocates a boat with a top speed of 150.

Fruits#

Fruits will spawn in the game every 30 minutes around the map. There is a slim 0.1% chance to attain Fruit from a chest. However, players can always  buy this end-game item from the Merchant. This item is stored in a player’s inventory and won’t be lost if they die. There are numerous types of Fruits available in the game. A list of available Fruits in A One Piece Game Trello & Wiki is given below.

A One Piece Game NPC Trello & Wiki#

Set Spawn#

This NPC sets the spawnpoint.

Rayleigh#

This NPC can be found in the Random Wilderness and can tech players Haki for 700K Beli.

Merchant#

Players can find this NPC in Logue Town and buy random fruits for 25M Beli.

Business man#

Also located in Logue Town, this NPC allows players to remove their current Devil Fruit for 5M Beli or Robux.

Blacksmith#

This NPC will take weapons.

Traveling Merchant#

Players can get a Island Tracker Compass from this NPC that can point out Islands for 50,000 Beli.

Xury#

This NPC sells quest scrolls that can give players an extra quest slot. Players can get up to 5 quest slots in total. Located at #1 Starter Island, this NPC will give the 1st scroll for 500K Beli. The 2nd scroll can be purchased for 1M Beli. Players can purchase the 3rd and the 4th scroll for 1.5M Beli and 2M Beli respectively.

Fosc#

Players can get the dual flintlock for 175,000 Beli from this NPC. This NPC can be found at #1 Starter Island.

Xury’s Brother#

Located on #1 Starter Island, this NPC sells players the flintlock for 75,000 Beli.

Quest Dummy#

These NPCs will ask players to help fight enemies and allow them to choose their quest from the Quest menu.

Sanji#

A fighting style teacher, Sanji can be found on #1 Starter Island. This NPC teaches players Black Leg for 500K Beli.

Zoro#

As might be evident from the name, Zoro is a Sword Style teacher that can be found on #2 Shell’s Island. His lessons cost from 100K to 5M Beli.

Fishman#

Found in #4 Arlong Park, this NPC can teach players Fishman Karate for 600K Beli.

Carrot#

This NPC teaches players Electro Fighting Style for 550K Beli and can be found on #6 Skypiea.

Fighting Style#

There are 4 fighting styles available in the game. Here is the A One Piece Game Trello & Wiki on each of these styles.

Combat#

This is the basic fighting style that players get when starting out in the game. It only has one move and cannot be reverted back to once players upgrade to a better fighting style.

Fishman Karate#

This fighting style is great for PvP and has ranged moves. Players can use attack Shark Bullets to create mini sharks that explode on impact. Moreover, players can increase their skills damage and swim in the ocean with a Devil Fruit by using the Fishman attack. The other Fishman Karate attacks, Thousand Brick Fist and Kaimen Wari, are also capable of great chaos. While the Thousand Brick Fist cause a mid explosion on impact, players can create a punch shockwave with Kaimen Wari. The fighting style can be obtained by talking to Fisherman.

Electro#

This is a high damage, high speed fighting style. Players can teleport to their cursor using this style. Also, using the Electro Smash, players can create a damage dealing lighting shockwave that can be activated by jumping and smashing the ground on the cursor location. Moreover, the Electro Discharge can create lighting over a certain AOE. The Electro Rush attack allows players to rush and deal high speed damage. Lastly, the Electricity attack can can players hands to electrify and deal added damage. This fighting Style can be acquired by talking to Carrot.

Black Leg#

The last fighting technique is a great idea if players are planning to grind in A One Piece Game. It has a high AOE and medium Base Damage. Players can also opt for high damage with the Diable Jambe where players attacks cause burn damage. Like the Electro, this fighting style also has the option to teleport players to where their mouse is pointing with the Anti Manner Kick Course move. In addition, players can jump and smash the ground with Mouton Shot for an extremely powerful dropkick. This fighting style can be purchased by talking to Sanji.

Haki#

The Haki is a mysterious power in A One Piece Game that can allow players to use their spiritual energy for a number of objectives. Let us take a look at the Haki available in the game and how to get them.

Armament Haki#

This Haki allows players to create an invisible armor that offers great offensive and defensive buffs. Moreover, it can be used to injure Logia users that are immune to ordinary physical attacks. This Haki can be obtained from Rayleigh for 700K Beli.

Observation Haki#

This Haki unlocks at 100 Haki stat. It allows users to develop a sixth sense and gain limited precognitive abilities. With the help of this Haki, players can dodge every attack in the game.

Thunder God#

Usopp#

Located in the Syrup Island, this Boss goves a 5% chance of a Slingshot drop.

Desert Bandit Leader#

Magellan#

Doflamingo#

The captain of the Donquixote Pirates.

Luffy#

Players have a 1% chance of learning his 2nd gear ability after taking him down.

Caesar Clown#

1% chance of gas fruit drop.

Akainu#
