Warzone Fractured Intel Mission Locations Ghost Message Location Evacuations Were Underway And More Explained

January 4, 2023 · 4 min · 793 words · Robert Paulsen

Released in the first week of Season 4, these have you find items on the map while playing matches by following a series of clues. Completing each one rewards you with XP, while the entire set rewards you with 10,000XP - giving you a boost towards completing your Season 4 Battle Pass. Currently, as of Warzone Season 4, you can no longer complete this activity. If you’re looking for more help, check out our on-going best Warzone weapons page and the Vanguard Royale meta.
Each Fractured Intel step must be completed in sequence. You can find it within the Challenges area of the Warzone menu, then tab to the end to find these objectives. Remember these need to be done in Warzone matches. If you’re struggling to grab certain items because the areas are heavily populated - the Intel locations are the same for everyone, after all - then try landing near the location and give it a few minutes before moving in, or play until you enter the Gulag and use that second match appearance to pay a visit (assuming you win, of course). Once done, you can move onto the Hunting the Enemy Intel and Hidden Cargo Intel. To complete this, visit the TV Station, the named location in the middle of the map. You specifically need to go to the news room area, which is on the ground floor on the main building. Look at the desk and you will see the Intel. Press the button prompt and it’ll be yours. If you’re struggling to do this Intel quickly - the TV Station is a popular location - then try parachuting through the broken segment of roof to the ground floor, then going through the double doors and taking a right, where the desk will be in front of you. To complete this, visit the Airport, the named location in the west of the map. You need to visit the north side of the main terminal building, to where you can see a corridor leading out of the top of the building. The easiest way to get this is to parachute in through the broken part of the roof nearby, which will drop you next to the objective. Interact with the desk when prompted to move onto the next Fractioned step. This step has you revisit the Airport, but this time, you must visit the control tower just north of the main building. You have to reach the top floor of the tower. To do this, you must start on the ground floor, and use the elevator shaft inside to zipline up - you cannot enter by parachuting in from above. From there, take the steps up, and on your right is where you can interact and collect the intel. This location has you find a plane crash site in the north of the map. Specifically, you need to go directly south of the Military base, where there is a forested area. You’ll find the crashed plane here - it’s thankfully pretty visible when parachuting in. The plane has broken into sections, and you need to go to the nose (or front) of the plane, which is to the west. Look among the seats and you’ll find the intel. This objective has you go to the Military Base proper, which again, is to the north. The Military Base is a location formed mostly of individual buildings, but you’ll want to find the largest one just off the centre. From here, go to the building next to it in the south-west. Inside on the ground floor is the intel on the desk. This objective has you go to the Military Base once more, which again, is in the north of the map. Visit the south-west most building here - it’s in its own fenced off compound next to a large tower. Inside this corner building are two monitors on a desk and the Intel location.
