Warzone Battle Of Verdansk Event Rewards And Vanguard Event End Time Explained

November 29, 2022 · 5 min · 922 words · Louis Bingman

Titled as the Battle of Verdansk, this event takes place entirely in Warzone during Season 5, providing a first worldwide look to the upcoming entry in the series, which is situated in the World War II era. This page will cover the Call of Duty: Vanguard reveal release time as well as how Warzone’s Battle of Verdansk event works. Currently, as of Warzone Season 4, you can no longer complete this activity. If you’re looking for more help, check out our on-going best Warzone weapons page and the Vanguard Royale meta.
On this page: The start times are as follows:

UK: 6:30pm (BST) Europe: 7:30pm (CEST) East Coast US: 1:30pm (EDT) West Coast US: 10:30am (PDT)

Battle of Verdansk will be available until Monday, August 23rd. The end times are as follows:

UK: 6pm (BST) Europe: 7pm (CEST) East Coast US: 1pm (EDT) West Coast US: 10am (PDT)

This reveal will take place within Warzone as an in-game event under the Battle of Verdansk premise. As per usual with season launches, be prepared for the release times to change in case there’s any last minute changes or setbacks from the developer. The event kicks off as a separate playlist in Warzone. A total of 32 players (eight squads) all matchmake in the same lobby, and the match is entirely PvE (yes, even before the game commences.) From here the first step is simple: all squads must head to the same spot and prepare the ambush for the incoming armored train. This is done by planting TNT at the marked location on the map. After all groups have finished doing this, a countdown will indicate when the vehicle is about to appear. The goal is simple: all 32 players must join forces together to take down the armored train at all costs, destroying the turrets on each of the cars while simultaneously damaging the cars themselves on specific occasions. You’re granted an assault rifle and a rocket launcher by default (you can revive indefinitely, and the loadout will change each time), but there are different explosives and other weapons such as LMGs and grenade launchers to loot. Of course, this is a moving train we’re talking about, which means there’ll be plenty of vehicle options around for you to tag along. It’s likely that you’ll see other players flying helicopters or driving trucks, so always keep an eye on these opportunities to jump in any available seat. Keep in mind that the train will attack back with its turrets. During a few moments of the match, it will also begin shooting explosive rounds, which can be quite deadly even if you’re fully armored. Don’t worry, though, as you will respawn in a manner of seconds, and there’s no real penalty. It’s worth noting that the total squad damage will impact on how many rewards you get, so it’s best to focus fire as early as possible to maximize the time. The train will slowly start losing turrets, as well as carts (you can always see its current state in a graph at the top of the screen). At one point, after all cars have been destroyed, the locomotive will be destroyed. During the final push, the train will begin to move slower than before, up to the point of being immobilized in front of a gas station. There, everyone can just give it with everything they got until it explodes. Congrats! You have completed the Battle of Verdansk. But there is one step left. Bombs will suddenly begin dropping from the sky, and it’s time to make a run for it. You can’t use any vehicles at this point, so prepare to sprint towards the marked location. (In our experience, the sprint button would sometimes stop working during the match - while it may have been just a temporary bug, it’s worth keeping in mind.) From here, whether or not you make it to the marked spot is indifferent, as the mission will end up in the same way. That being said, if you manage to signal for exfil, you’ll obtain an extra reward (which can be seen in the following section.) All that is left to do is to sit back, relax, and watch the trailer for Call of Duty: Vanguard. Warzone Season 5 is almost here! If you want to learn more about firepower from previously, we explain the best Welgun loadout, best Cooper Carbine loadout, best Gorenko Anti-Tank Rifle loadout, best STG44 loadout, best MP40 loadout, best Automaton loadout, best BAR loadout, best Combat Shotgun loadout, best Type 100 loadout, and best Kar98k loadouts. These are primarily unlocked by doing damage to the armored train. The total amount is shared amongst the squad, as well as the rewards, so it’s best to organize the group so everyone is always shooting at the target. The Battle of Verdansk rewards and requirements are the following:

Republic Impaler (Epic Knife Blueprint): 30,000 total squad damage

Dogfight (Epic Sticker): 45,000 total squad damage

Timetable (Epic Watch): 60,000 total squad damage

Clashing Sides (Epic Emblem): 75,000 total squad damage

Siberian Strider (Epic Motorcycle Skin): 100,000 total squad damage

Warbound (Legendary Calling Card): 135,000 total squad damage

Little Shark (Epic Charm): Signal for exfil at the end of the Battle of Verdansk

Dark Forest (Legendary Light Machine Gun Bravo Blueprint): Finish the Battle of Verdansk

Remember you can replay the event as many times as you like before the end date, giving you multiple chances to unlock all the above rewards. Best of luck out there!
