Grim Dawn Build and Mastery Guide#

Without future ado, let’s check out some of the best Masteries in the game.

Grim Dawn – Arcanist#

The Arcanist is similar to a  “mage-like” character in Grim Dawn. They believe that the manifestation of magic is not some unexplainable mystery or the will of the gods, but a science.  Arcanists can take advantage of Fire, Cold, Lightning, and Aether elemental damage. And with the right choice, you will be able to take Chaos damage. Certain skills will require a caster off-hand to equip. They tend to stay at a distance during the battle and are best to be paired with a one-handed gun.

Grim Dawn – Demolitionist#

Pyrotechnic masters of the imperial army; Demolitionists are part engineer; part sorcerer. They are the king of Fire damage. With a barrage of explosives, traps, and munitions they can beat even the strongest of enemies. Their balanced growth and lack of weapon dependency will open the build for either melee or ranged combat.. They also provide a pretty damn good utility with skills like Flashbang.

Grim Dawn – Nightblade#

Nightblades were clandestine warriors that sold their services to the great houses of the empire. They excel in all kinds of martial weapons and are feared for their deadly blade magic. They have a  variety of illusory blades and prefer surgical strikes to prolonged fights. They specialize in Physical, Cold, or Poison damage depending on their setup. Typically Nightblades make use of a two one-handed weapon that moves as if it’s dancing in combat.

Grim Dawn – Occultist#

Occultist’s craft focuses heavily on summoning and borrowed powers granted by the three witch gods Bysmiel, Solael, and Dreeg. They specialize in inflicting curses and spells that inflict damage with poison, acid, and entropic energy. These builds will be focusing on dealing damage to your enemies. While it is doing so you will have to ensure your safety and keep summoning pets and fighting.

Shamans are capable of wielding the terrible forces of nature against their foes or even calling upon savage beasts to come to their aid. Grim Dawn – Soldier#

Soldiers prefer the use of close combat weaponry, such as a sword and shield, but can also prove formidable with firearms. They are not flashy but do make up for practicality. Soldiers can be used as a subclass for their strong passives and bonuses as well as a tank or heavy melee hitter, and a firearm build depending on the build you wanna make.

Grim Dawn – Necromancer#

The Necromancers of Cairn seek balance through research and mastery over the ultimate fate that awaits all mortals: death. Necromancers make heavy use of conjuring forth skeletal minions and sapping the very vitality from their unwary foes. They can also elect to skip pets entirely and instead focus on necrotic spells to deal Vitality and Aether damage with some Cold and Poison added.
