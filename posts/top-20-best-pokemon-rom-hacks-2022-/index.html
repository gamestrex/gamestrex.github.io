2. Pokémon Outlaw#

15. Pokemon Radical Red#

This is both a variation and a hack of Pokemon FireRed. With this game, you should expect a bumpy ride and nerve-wracking challenges. The gameplay is challenging but satisfying. The main attributes of the game are:

EV training areas Coloured IV from the beginning itself Modified stats, abilities, moves, etc.

16. Pokemon Flora Sky#

Pokemon Flora sky adds a lot to the OG Pokemon Emerald. With a whopping 360 new pokemons and some of them having new moves as well, there will be a lot of new things for you to discover. The USP of the ROM is how it changes the whole structure of the game. Players won’t have to grind their way through all the gyms and city’s instead they will have to find and locate things to advance further in the game. This fixes the monotony of the game and makes it more interesting. The ROM also introduces more items in the game and makes catching Rotom a bit easier.

17. Pokemon Light Platinum#

Pokemon Light Platinum adds the Zhery Region to the Pokemon Ruby game. Players can interact and challenge the new gym leaders of the Zhery Region and also catch pokemon from the Sinnoh, Hoenn, Kanto, and Jhoto regions. The ROM will also add some new special events and the Pokemon World Championship as well.

18. Pokemon Mega Power#

While other ROM’s add new features and regions to the game Mega Power changes the direction of the OG Pokemon Emerald. The characters start experimenting on the Pokemons to make the strongest pokemon. Experimentations cost money and hence the need for funds arises. A Financer comes around to help them but asks them to go a shady quest for him. To know what does the Financer wants and how things turn out you’ll have to play the game. The ROM also adds three new regions to Pokemon Emerald and all the Mega evolutions of the generations IV, V, VI, and VII.

19. Pokemon Mega Emerald X and Y#

As the name suggests Mega Emerald X and Y is a simple yet fun ROM to have in Pokemon Emerald. The ROM adds almost all the elements of Pokemon X and Y to Pokemon Emerald, from Pokemons of gen I to VI to all the Legendary pokemon. There is a lot for fans of the Pokemon franchise. This is just a big Pokemon game that players should try.

20. Pokemon Dark Cry#

As the name suggests the ROM takes a dark turn in the world of Pokemon hacks. You wake up in a cavern and see a person with an “R” on his shirt. Suspecting he is from Team rocket you try to chase him but aren’t able to catch him. An earthquake follows and a shadow keeps getting closer to you, when it gets very close and things go dark you wake up again in the same cavern only to realize it was all a dream. Soon you realize your dream is becoming the reality and things start getting spookier. Players start in a new region called Trion, where you’ll have to read the Pokepaper to get new quests and side-quests and advance further in the game.
