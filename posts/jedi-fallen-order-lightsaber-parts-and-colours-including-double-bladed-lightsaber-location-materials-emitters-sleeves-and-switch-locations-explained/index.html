Jedi Fallen Order Lightsaber Parts And Colours Including Double Bladed Lightsaber Location Materials Emitters Sleeves And Switch Locations Explained

December 12, 2022 · 22 min · 4651 words · Marie Alt

The emitters, switches, sleeves and even the lightsaber material can be swapped and changed at any lightsaber crafting table you find. These parts are mostly found in chests hidden on the numerous planets you explore. However, some can be found upon interacting with a new crafting table, while others are unlocked during specific points in the story. On this page: Several parts require specific abilities to be unlocked before they are obtainable. So, to save you having to travel back and forth from planet to planet every time you get a new power, it’s recommended that you complete the story first and make final return trip when you are able to grab them all hassle-free. As such, expect location spoilers throughout the page. The parts will be listed in order of which order they appear in their category, so that you can look up which piece you are missing and go out to grab that specifically. Interact with this table to have Cal repair his busted weapon and gain the ability to use the double sided lightsaber. Great for clearing out groups of enemies, the double sided lightsaber comes with its own set of attacks and skills you can gain through meditation. You can activate it manually by pressing right on the d-pad, or through certain unlockable skills that let you flow through single and double sided styles at through attacks during combat. Once you reach the cave with your new Kyber crystal inside, look to the right and you’ll see a table made of ice. Interacting with this table lets you change the colour of your lightsaber and grants you this new style. Rather than gaining a whole new set of moves like with the dual wielded lightsaber, you instead gain two super-powerful attacks that can be triggered by pressing Left Bumper / L1 and X / Square. These are great for taking down tougher opponents such as purge troopers, and deals massive amounts of damage to both health and block bars. If you want to know where exactly, read on… Purple / Yellow / Cyan / Magenta / Indigo lightsaber color locations At the tail end of the story you’ll travel to Ilum to repair your lightsaber in the old Jedi temple there. At the end of this cave, you obtain a new Kyber crystal and get to choose its colour. You can choose between the original two colours (blue or green), or you can choose a selection of new colours. Regardless of the choice you make here, you can switch the colour of your lightsaber to any of these new options at any lightsaber crafting table afterwards. Peace and Justice Emitter location After you’ve defeated the squad of stormtroopers in the Shadowlands on Kashyyyk, you’ll swing across a large hole and onto some vines. Climb up this vine wall and you’ll reach some more water with a large root hanging over it. Jump into the water and dive. Swim to the left and you’ll come across a chest with the Peace and Justice emitter inside. Valour and Wisdom Emitter location Start at the save point in the Subterranean Refuge on Bogano, then enter the Great Divide. Force push the bridge to your right and walk across. Once across, continue forward and enter the large illuminated cave ahead of you. There should be a small island inside with a bogling hole on it, swim to the left side of this island then dive directly down. Once you reach the bottom of the water you will find a chest with the emitter inside. Power and Control Emitter location Starting from the save point in the Swamp of Sacrifice on Dathomir, run up the tree branch and drop down to the other side of the swamp. Once you’re back down, you’ll have the option to go left, right, or straight. Go straight, and you will find yourself in a large open area with undead nightsisters and a lesser nydack. Once they are all gone, walk towards the cliff and there will be a locked chest. Have BD-1 open it and the emitter is yours. Passion and Strength Emitter location Start off at the save point in the Imperial refinery near the giant mixer on Kashyyyk. Cross over the mixer, climb up to the hallway above and walk left. Exit the hallway into the area filled with stormtroopers, kill them all, and proceed forward. Sitting by a ledge will be a chest BD-1 must slice, with the emitter inside. Elemental Nature Emitter location Starting from the Crash Site save point on Zeffo, jump into the water and swim forward. Eventually, you’ll come across some land with two Phillaks walking around and a large circular piece of wreckage above it. Vault up and walk past the right side of a large rock there. At the other side of this rock will be more water. Jump back into the water and dive down to the bottom. Then, look right to find the chest with the emitter inside. Duty and Resolve Emitter location Travel into the Derelict Hanger from the Landing Pad on Zeffo. Eventually, you will reach a courtyard with four Stormtroopers. After killing them, turn around and walk alongside the right side of the building towards a stone wall. Once there, jump onto the metal platform above you to find a chest with the emitter inside. Eno Cordova Emitter location Start at the Abandoned Workshop save point on Bogano and take the metal pipe to Hermit’s Abode. Make your way up to the second floor via the stairs then walk left of the yellow rope. Jump over a small gap to find a chest with the Eno Cordova Emitter inside. Magus Emitter location Starting from the Landing Pad on Dathomir, climb up to the upper Strangled cliffs and make your way to the rope shortcut by the three bane back spiders. From the rope, follow the path towards the two large statues while hugging the stone to your right. Take the first path right you come across, and you’ll find a locked chest. Have BD-1 unlock it, and the emitter will be inside. Here is where to find all Lightsaber Switch locations: Travel to Kashyyyk and take the long zipline down from Kyyyalstaad Falls into Kyyyalstaad Basin. Once you hit the water, immediately dive underwater. Turn to your left and swim over a giant tree root and you’ll find a chest surrounded by underwater plants. Peace and Justice II Switch location Start from the Origin Lake save point and jump down into the water. Look for the small platform with a single lung planet near the save point, then dive below it to find a chest with this lightsaber part inside. Valour and Wisdom Switch location Starting from the save point in the Subterranean Refuge on Bogano, enter the Great Divide and walk right. Force push the bridge down if you haven’t already, and cross over to the other side. From there, travel straight ahead until you come to an illuminated cave. Enter this cave, then dive underwater. Once at the bottom, swim alongside the left wall and you’ll find a chest containing the switch amongst some lights. Valour and Wisdom II Switch location Start at the save point in the Subterranean Refuge save point on Bogado, then enter the Great Divide. Follow the path to your right and wall run along the metal surface ahead of you. Continue straight ahead towards the zipline, then look left to find the Valour and Wisdom II switch inside the chest. Power and Control Switch location From the central Crystal Caves save point on Ilum, jump over onto the stone you forced pushed down during the quest ‘Find your Kyber Crystal’ and climb up to a higher platform. From there, jump down into the water below and dive. Underneath the water, you’ll find a chest with the switch inside. Power and Control II Switch location From the Swamp of Sacrifice save point on Dathomir, walk up the branch and jump down at the other side of the swamp by the crashed ship. Climb up a vine wall to ascend to the upper level, then walk over the branches until you drop down into a small room. In that room will be a chest with the power and control switch inside. Passion and Strength Switch location To find this part head to the Overgrown Pass save point on Kashyyyk. Take the path back towards the Imperial Refinery via a climbable wall and a pipe you must force push down. Once you’ve crossed the bridge, turn right and walk across the pipe back into the Overgrown Pass. Continue onward over a metal walkway and stay left as you go. You will come to a small area with two flame beetles and a slyyyg. Kill them, them look to your right to find a chest with the switch inside. Passion and Strength II Switch location Start at the Overgrown Pass save point on Kashyyyk and swing across to the Kyyyalstaad Falls. Proceed forward until you kill a purge trooper commander. Once you do turn left and walk through the protective barrier so that you are overlooking the jungle. Then look left and you’ll see a wall you can run across leading to a hidden platform. Land on this platform and to your left will be a broken door you can force push through. Past the door is the chest with the switch inside. Elemental Nature Switch location Start from the Crash Site save point on Zeffo, then jump into the water. Swim left , and climb up on the large landmass that leads up to the broken wing. From there, keep walking forward until you reach the edge of the water. Jump back in then dive down, from there you should see a small cave directly in front of you. Inside this cave is the chest with the lightsaber switch inside. Elemental Nature II Switch location Start from the save point in the Venator Wreckage on Zeffo. Jump in the water and swim towards the crashed republic dropship. Then, swim left to find a hole in the wreckage. Climb through this hole, and you’ll find a chest with the switch inside. Duty and Resolve Switch location Make your way up through the Weathered Monument on Zeffo until you fight a rocket trooper on a slope. Enter the dark bunker behind them, then proceed through to the other side. You’ll be attacked by two scout troopers on a balcony looking over the water. Once you kill them both, look to the left of the balcony to find a chest by the railing. You can find the duty and resolve switch inside this chest. Duty and Resolve II Switch location Starting from the Weathered Monument save point on Zeffo, walk towards the wall you can run across nearby. Instead of running along it, drop down and there will be a locked door BD-1 can slice into. Have them do so, and you’ll find a chest with the switch inside. Eno Cordova Switch location For this part, start at the Abandoned Workshop save point on Bogano and cross the metal pipe to enter Hermit’s Abode. Once inside, squeeze through the broken door to enter a dark room. Within this room is an electrical panel BD-1 can overcharge. Have them do so, and drop down into the hidden compartment in the middle of the room. There you’ll find a chest containing the lightsaber switch. Magus Switch location Start off from the Brother’s Basion save point on Dathomir, then walk along the path upwards until you drop down into a room with a bane back spider and a nightbrother warrior inside. After they are both gone, look to the left side of the room to find a chest with the lightsaber switch inside it. Here is where to find all Lightsaber Sleeve locations: This lightsaber part is gained automatically when you gain the ability to use a double bladed lightsaber. Travel to the Chieftain’s Canopy at the top of the origin tree on Kashyyyk and you’ll find a lightsaber table made of stone. Interact with this table to gain your new lightsaber and the peace and justice sleeve. Peace and Justice II Sleeve location Go to the save point in the Fractured Plane on Bogano, then climb up the vines to the higher level. Once you have, jedi flip over the gap to the other side of the broken walkway to your right. Once you’ve crossed over, walk left until you find the chest with the sleeve inside. Valour and Wisdom Sleeve location Once you reach the Shadowlands on Kashyyyk, you’ll climb up to a clearing where stormtroopers will be waiting for you. Once you defeat them, look to you left and you should see a small body of water and a waterfall. Jump into the water, then swim left until you reach a chest. This chest will have the valour and wisdom lightsaber sleeve inside. Valour and Wisdom II Sleeve location Start from the save point in the Great Divide on Bogano. Swing across the vine nearby and climb the vines to the two stone statues. Then head to the left of these and jump down to a metal platform below. Using either of the paths ahead of you, proceed up the rocky platform until you reach the top. There, you will find a chest containing the lightsaber sleeve. Power and Control Sleeve location To find this part, start at the Collapsed Settlement save point on Dathomir and start climbing upwards. Eventually, you will cross over twisted roots and reach a higher platform with an elevator to your left. Take the elevator down, and you’ll find a chest with the part next to a shortcut door. Power and Control II Sleeve location Start from the save point in the Crystal Caves on Ilum. From there follow the path between the two statues. Take a right, then continue forward until you enter a large cave with two probe droids inside. In this cave, hug the left wall until you come across a locked chest, This chest has the power and control II sleeve waiting for you. Passion and Strength Sleeve location From the Kyyyalstaad Basin save point on Kashyyyk, turn towards the giant zipline and enter the water. Swim while hugging the left wall around the water, until you reach a destroyed wookie hut. This hut has a platform you can climb on, with the sleeve inside. Passion and Strength II Sleeve location To get this part, travel to the sap mixer (the spinning platform) in the imperial refinery on Kashyyyk . Cross over the platform to the panel where BD-1 can overcharge the mixer, then take the path next to this panel where you can find an elevator. Take the elevator down, and you’ll find a chest with the lightsaber sleeve inside. Elemental Nature Sleeve location This lightsaber part is right next to the save point at the Crash Site on Zeffo. From here, jump into the water and turn back around toward the save point. Then dive down under water and you’ll see a small cave hidden in the rock with a chest inside. This chest, once opened, will provide the lightsaber sleeve. Elemental Nature II Sleeve location Go to the Weathered Monument save point on Zeffo. Follow the path upwards and you’ll pass by a heavy storm trooper who will shoot at you while you cross over some metal pipes. After you get past him, proceed forward and climb up a yellow rope. Once you reach the top of the rope, jump onto the platform in front of you and head right round a hidden path. Along this path is a chest perched on top of a large rectangular stone on your right side with the sleeve inside. Duty and Resolve Sleeve location In the Abandoned Village on Zeffo, climb up onto the rooftops via a metal platform near the large stone steps. Cross the metal pipe and continue over the buildings until you come to a circular yellow platform. Jump over this platform to the building behind it, then turn right. In front of you will be a chest containing the lightsaber sleeve. Duty and Resolve II Sleeve location Travel to the Windswept Ruins save point on Zeffo. Jump down and head to the room’s north eastern corner (with the tomb entrance being north). Inside a small room behind a fallen pillar is a hidden chest, with the duty and resolve sleeve inside. Eno Cordova Sleeve location To obtain this part, start from the Subterranean Refuge save point on Bogano and exit to the Great Divide. Once there, look right and walk towards a bridge that you must force push to cross over to the other side of the area. Once across, walk forward and enter the large cave ahead of you. Once in this cave, climb up the vines and walk halfway up the stairs. There, you’ll find a door that BD-1 will have to slice into to unlock. After the door is opened, walk into the room and interact with the crafting table to obtain this lightsaber sleeve. Magus Sleeve location From the Brother’s Bastion save point, walk up the stairs and through the shortcut door. Walk left and down a smaller of stairs, then take another left at the elevator. There, you will find a lightsaber workbench. Interacting with it for the first time will provide this lightsaber sleeve. Here is where to find all Lightsaber Material locations: Go to the Imperial Headquarters save point on Zeffo. Take the elevator nearby up to the higher level, then turn left and enter the room in front of you. There, you’ll find a bridge you can pull down. Cross it, then climb up to a hidden platform. Force slow the fan ahead of where you’re standing, then jump across. Once done, you will find a chest with the material inside. Matte Material location For this part, you’ll need to return to the Kashyyyk Imperial refinery save point by the large mixer/ Cross the mixer and climb up to the hallway above. Take a right, and and make your way to the room with stormtroopers and flame beetles fighting each other. Rather than jumping down, turn right and cross the metal beam to find a locked door. Splice the door open, and you’ll find a chest with the material inside. Electrum Plated Material location Start from the save point in Origin Lake on Kashyyyk. Jump into the water, and swim towards the small platform nearby with a single lung fish on it. Once you reach it, swim to the right of the platform until you reach a dead end. Once you do, dive underwater to find a tunnel entrance directly in front of you. Swim into this tunnel to find a chest with the material inside. Copper Material location Start from the Abandoned Village save point on Zeffo and enter the dark cave nearby. You’ll reach a split path. Head down the left path until you cross a metal pipe. Once you do, jump the gap on your left side to reach a chest with the Copper Material inside. Durasteel Material location Walk to the entrance to the Auger Pulverizers on Zeffo via the Weathered Monument save point. Enter the cave leading up the mountain, where these pulverisers will be in your way. Instead of passing the first pulveriser you come across, force slow it as it retracts out of your way and follow it. As you follow it backwards, you’ll see a hidden room to your left with a chest inside. Here you can grab the durasteel material, just be careful not to get squished on the way out. Polished Bronzium Material location Starting from the Crash Site save point on Zeffo, jump in the water and swim directly ahead to the large circular island with the jotaz patrolling around it. Vault onto this island, then stick right as you walk across it. Keep walking past the crashed ship and the rope until you reach water again. Once you do, jump into the water and dive. When you hit the bottom, swim directly ahead to find a chest with the material inside. Dolovite Material location Enter the Nightbrother Dungeon on Dathomir via the wooden beam underneath the bridge. To the right of the room will be a tunnel. Go through it, then turn left at the other side. The chest with the material will be there next to a lit torch. Doonium Material location From the outdoors Imperial Refinery save point on Kashyyyk (near the spiders), jump down to the area filled with stormtroopers. Once you’ve killed all the enemies there, hug the left wall until you find a chest with the part inside. Chromium Material location On the top floor of the Venator Wreckage on Zeffo you’ll come across a room with two stormtroopers fighting a jotaz. In this room, straight ahead from the door you enter from, is a locked chest BD-1 has to slice. Inside this chest is the Chronium lightsaber material. Duralium Material location Start from the Origin Lake save point on Kashyyyk. Run across the wall next to the save point and you’ll land in the water. In front of you should be a lung plant perched on a rock in the water. Dive down into the water, and swim past the lungfish. You should find a chest below with the lightsaber material inside. Osmiridium Material location Upon leaving the Tomb of Eilram for the first time, air vents will open up around the Windswept Ruins on Zeffo. Look to the north-eastern part of the area (with the entrance of the ruins being north). There, you will see two air vents close to each other next to a large curved rock. Use these air vents to ascend to a broken bridge and you will find a chest with the lightsaber material inside. Durite Material location For this lightsaber part, return to the Abandoned Village save point on Zeffo and walk back down into the village. From there, take the left path alongside a building until you come across a locked chest BD-1 with have to slice. Once opened, you’ll get the material. Cerakote Material location Starting from the Icy Caves save point on Zeffo, run up the ramp to the large circular clearing then take the yellow rope down to the rocky path below. Head right past a Phillak towards the elevator where two stormtroopers are standing. Kill them both, then look past the elevator to see a path where you can wall run. Follow this path until you come to a small room where a scout trooper and a purge trooper are waiting. After you manage to beat them, open the chest to your left to find the lightsaber material. Orichalc Material location Go to the room where you fight the Rabid Jotaz in the Broken Wing on Zeffo, then jump down and force push your way through a broken door. Continue down this path until you reach a grate you must break through. Do so, then jump down. You’ll enter a dark room filled with scazzs. Kill them all before having BD-1 overcharge a panel and open a door. Proceed through this door past some Skungus until you reach a flooded corridor. Jump into the water and dive down. Once you’re underwater, swim forward and you’ll find a chest with the material inside. Slivian Iron Material location Start from the room where you first learn to force push in the Tomb of Eilram on Zeffo. From there, look to the right side of the room to find a small doorway blocked by a block you can push. Move this block out of your way, then continue up the stairs until you reach yet another block that you can push into a room below. Do so, then jump down. From there, push the block towards the wall to your left so that you can climb up, and you’ll find the lightsaber part in a chest next to a force echo. Duraplast Material location To get this material start from the save point in the Imperial Trench on Ilum. From there, look left of the cuttable rocks to the climbable wall you can jump to. Jump on this wall, and climb up to the ledge above. On this ledge is a chest with the duraplast material inside. Hasian Smelt Material location For this part, travel to the Tomb of Miktrull on Zeffo and climb up to the second floor of the large circular building. You can find this near the first save point following the Second Sister fight. Once there, walk to the right side of the room and jump on some vines to ascend to a higher level. Follow the path left around the room to find a broken wall you can force push. Break through this wall and you can find a chest with the lightsaber material inside. Corundum Material location Start from the Great Divide save point and swing over towards the vines. Climb these vines and you’ll arrive at the two Zeffo statues. Walk straight and jump down to the metal platform towards below. From there, climb up the structure until you reach the top. Once at the peak, walk to the left side facing the giant creature to find a locked chest BD-1 will have to slice into. Once the chest is unlocked, the material can be found inside. Crodium Material location Ride the giant zipline down to the Kyyyalstaad basin on Kashyyyk into a body of water. As soon as you hit the water, turn left and swim towards a destroyed wookie hut. Climb up, and you’ll find a chest with the lightsaber material inside. Arcetron Material location To find this lightsaber part, start at the Ice Caves save point on Zeffo. From there, ascend the ramp leading up to the large platform where the jotaz is walking about. Run to the other side of this platform and slide down the rope. Then, jump up the ledge to your left and cross over the spinning saw blades. Once you’re across, kill the two stormtroopers waiting for you before finding a locked chest by the elevator. Have BD-1 slice this chest and the material is yours. Lamina Steel Material location Start from the Abandoned Workshop save point on Bogano. Cross over, then walk left until you reach the edge of the Bogdo Sinkholes. Jump off the cliff and force pull a rope towards you so you can swing over to the top of the Abandoned Workshop. From there, walk forward towards the direction of the temple and you’ll come across a chest that BD-1 will have to slice into. Once unlocked, open the chest to gain the material. Bronzium Material location Enter the Abandoned Village on Kashyyyk and take the huge slive all the way down into a large body of water. Once you’ve hit the water, dive and swim left. Continue swimming in that direction until you come across a chest with the material inside. Neuranium Material location From the Fractured Planes save point on Bogano, climb up the vines and jedi flip over the broken walkway to your right. From there, walk straight ahead until you reach a large gap you must jedi flip across. Once over the gap, you’ll see a chasm you must wall run over in order to proceed. After you’ve run across, you’ll be able to climb up a rock to find the hidden chest with the material inside.
