All Far Cry USB Songs (USB Sticks) Locations List#

Before you head to any location finding USB Songs in Far Cry 6, you must know that they are marked by a white USB icon on the minimap. When you get close to them, you will see both Red and Green illuminating. Since players are required to find 15 USB Sticks, players must know their exact locations to get their hands on all of them. With that in mind, we have created a guide showing the exact location of each USB Stick or Song in Far Cary 6. Before you embark on a journey to collect USB Songs in FC 6, I advise you to clear the map of Anti-Air Cannons. Once cleared, you will be able to use the Airdrop functions to get to each location in no time. Without any further ado, let’s find out where all USB Songs all located in Far Cry 6:

1. Hitboy#

The first USB Song is located inside a building of Viviro Nueve Plantation, which can be found in the southeast of Isla Santuario.

2. Pulpa De Tamarindo#

When you get to the above-mentioned location, you will find the second USB Stick on a table at Ocaso Marina.

3. Sentimiento Original#

The third USB Stick is located at Hideout Mambo. When you get to the location, you will have to find a Dumpster. When you get close to the Dumpster, you will see Sentimiento Original USB Stick is kept next to the Laptop.

4. Abuso De Poder#

Once you reach the above-mentioned location, you will have to get to the rooftop of the control tower, which is located at FDA Airbase Olimpia. Once you are on the rooftop, you will find this USB Stick attached to the Laptop.

5. What A Bam Bam#

Once you reach the town called Verdera, you can easily find this USB Stick.

6. Educate Ya#

After reaching the highlighted location, you will have to get inside the Gas Station. When you get into the station, you will find this USB Stick attached to the laptop.

7. Macarena#

When you get to this location, you will have to head towards the dumpster kept in the Parking lot. When you get there, you will find this USB Stick in a dumpster.

8. Dinero#

Once you have successfully reached the above-mentioned location, you will have to get to the rooftop of Orquidea Villa. You will have to climb up through the flowers hanging down behind the Villa. Once you get to the rooftop, you will find this USB Stick near the laptop.

9. Ay Lola “La Figura”#

Unlike the above-mentioned USB Sticks or Songs, getting your hands on Ay Lola ‘La Figura’ is a bit difficult and time-consuming. To obtain this USB Stick in Far Cry 6, you will have to complete Treasure Hunt. Once you complete the Treasure Hunt quest in FC6, you will be given this USB Stick as a reward.

10. La Bella Ciao De Libertad#

Once you get to the highlighted location, you will have to go find an entrance to the underground bunker, which can be found next to the Radio tower. When you go through the bunker, you will have to follow the path leading to this USB Stick.

11. Los Caminos De La Vida#

Once you reach the highlighted location, you will have a path that leads to this USB Stick. This USB Stick can be found on the patio behind a wooden house.

12. Yo Aprendi#

Once you land at this location, you simply need to get inside the hut located in Maldito Town. Once you get into the hut, you will find this USB Stick on the table.

13. La Vida Me Cambio#

After reaching the above-mentioned location, you will have to get inside the Guard-house, which is located at the entrance to FND Base “Sureno Shipyards”. As soon as you enter the guard-house, you will see this Stick lying on the table.

14. Pxssy Powah!#

To get this USB Stick in Far Cry 6, you will have to head to the Esperanza region. Once you get there, you will have to find the Guerilla Hideout “Mercurio Mecanico”. Once you reached the location, you will have to go upstairs to get your hands on this Stick.

15. Here We Kum#

To obtain the last USB Stick in Far Cry 6, you will have to move towards the balcony where the start was started. Now, you will have to follow the map that leads to this Stick. This USB Stick is located in the Storage room. That’s all you need to know about where to find all USB Sticks in Far Cry 6. Once you have successfully obtained all 15 of the USB Sticks (USB Songs) in FC 6, you will earn the That’s My Jam Trophy in the game.
