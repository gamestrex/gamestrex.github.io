How To Find Zealots In AC Valhalla#

Zealots are horse-riders and tough warriors that are always roaming in and around a specific region. While traveling you may even be able to accidentally stumble upon one of them. They are the ones that enforce the Order of the Ancients. Once you have reached within a certain radius of the Zealots a horn will begin to blow. This indicates that one of the 15th Zealots is nearby. Also, you can check the status of Zealots by opening the ‘Order’ page in your menu. Since the zealots have yet to be discovered by you their icon will be blacked out. Once you have come across a Zealot their icons will no longer be blacked out. After getting close enough to a zealot an icon will appear on the map. You will have to get very close to them to identify them till then you will only be seeing a black icon. This will leave a permanent mark on the map, This will be able to tell you their location at all times. When you defeat a Zealot you will successfully obtain an Order of the Ancients Medallion. You can deliver theses to  Hytham at the Hidden One’s Bureau at Ravensthorpe for a reward.

AC Valhalla Zealots Location#

Here we have given a list of all 15 Zealots and where to find them.

Beorthsige#

Location: Glowecestrescire Suggested Power: 280

Beorhtsige carries a long spear and likes to dye his beard with the blood of his victims. He tends to patrol the long roads to the East of the large Thieves Warren hill, and north of the central city of Glowcestre.

Bercthun#

Location: Hamtunscire Suggested Power: 340

You will be able to find him patrolling the outskirts of Wincestre by moving along the roads of Hamtunscire. He is a tough opponent and wields a two-handed axe which is quite dangerous. Few swings by him can instantly kill you so be careful around him.

Hrothgar#

Location: Suthsexe Suggested Power: 280

Hrothgar can be located in the Suthsexe region riding on the roads. He carries a Dane Axe but has an immensely powerful attack that is best to be blocked. It will do you well in focusing on dodging and coming up with hits.

Woden#

Location: Cent Suggested Power: 220

Woden can be on the roads of the Cent region on his horse. He has a lot of tricks up his skills alongside a quick fighting style. You will have to be quite careful as he attacks you. Defeating him will help you in receiving an item related to the legendary weapon, Excalibur.

Cudberct#

Location: Oxenefordscire Suggested Power: 160

You can find Cuberct in the Oxenefordscire region on a horse. His quick spear attacks will be a huge disadvantage to you as he packs some good combo attacks and making you run out of stamina.  Be careful of Cudberct’s poison grenades and focus on dodging along with attacks to deal damage on him.

Heike#

Location: Essexe Suggested Power: 250

Heike can be found in the Essexe region and carries a greatsword and shield into combat. Battling him with caution will be the best also do try isolating him from other soldiers by luring him away from the roads.

Wuffa#

Location: East Anglia Suggested Power: 160

Wuffa can be located in the left corner of East Anglia. You will be able to acquire a clue for The Ash-Spear after defeating him.

Kendall#

Location: Grantebridgescire Suggested Power: 90

Kendall wields a long sword and a shield and will be found in the area between Ravensburg and Walden, Granterbridgescire. Dodging and slowing time with the Brush with Death skill will prove to be effective against him.

Horsa#

Location: Oxenefordscire Suggested Power: 130

Horsa can be found in the Oxenefordscire region near the Alne River. He uses a huge Dane Axe and uses large sweeping strikes. He will most possibly hit you with a two-hit combo and strong or unblockable strikes. Attacking from a distance will be able to deal more damage on him and at the same time, you will even be able to avoid his hits.

Wealdmaer#

Location: Sciropescire Suggested Power: 160

Wealdmaer can be located in the Scropescire region and carries a spear for a fight. He will drop a random diamond-shaped rune and may even drop clues to the Order member The Dart.

Redwalda#

Location: Ledecestrescire Suggested Power: 90

You will be able to find Redwalda below the Ledecestrescire region, near the river,  on the main road of the area. He carries a  sword and a shield with him. If your power level is above 90 you will be able to defeat him with any problems.

Eorforwine#

Location: Grantebridgescire Suggested Power: 90

Eorforwine is found right on the middle road of the Grantebridgescire region. She tends to use an impenetrable shield and will throw multiple poison grenades. She also drinks a kind of liquid to regain her health it would be best to not let her have it to win the battle.

Osgar#

Location: Lincolnscire Suggested Power: 220

Osgar can be located on the left of the Lincolnscire region. He uses a spear for long-range attacks and has even summoned a wolf in between fights. Dodge and counter his attacks to assure your win.

Cola#

Location: Eurviscire and Lincolnscire Suggested Power: 90

Cola can be found at the top left side of the Ledecestrescire region, beside the bridge. Try isolating him from other soldiers by luring him away from the roads. Also, prevent him from drinking any kind of health potion.

Callin#

Location: Eurvicscire Suggested Power: 280

Callin can be located at the top right side of the map, in the Eurvicscire region. He carries a sword and bows with him and is the most annoying all zealots since you will have to keep dealing with poison grenades. Do not use poison on him since he seems immune to poison debuffs.
