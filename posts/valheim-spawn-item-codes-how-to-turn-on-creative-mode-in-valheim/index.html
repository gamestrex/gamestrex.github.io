Valheim Creative Mode Controls#

Turning On Creative Mode in Valheim is too simple. What all you need to is type “debugmode” in to the console. Make sure you have enabled cheats. You can enable cheats in Valheim by simply typing “imacheater” first. Once you enable Creative Mode, you can do the following things:

Hit “Z” to toggle Flying Mode Hit “K” to kill all surrounding enemies; Hit “B” to toggle No Placement Cost Mode.

The only reason why people want Creative Mode enabled is they can repair and construct anything without using Workbench or any cost. Upon combining this mode with the item Spawn Commands, you will be able to deep into the games’ assets.

How To Spawn Items In Valheim#

To spawn items in Valheim, you simply need to type “imacheater” (without quotes) in the console and then follow by spawn (itemName), (Amount) or (Level). If you want to spawn an item, you simply need to type the name of that item.  To spawn an entity or monster, you will have to type Bear followed by the level you want it to be.
