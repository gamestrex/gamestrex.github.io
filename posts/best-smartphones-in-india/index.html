Best Mobile Phones in India#

iPhone 11 Pro#

The iPhone 11 Pro is one of the topmost and most expensive mobile phones in India. It is powered by Apple’s latest A13 Bionic chip. You get an excellent 5.8-inch HDR10 display that tops at 800 nits. On the back lies a 12MP triple camera setup, that comes ingrained with a telephoto lens as well as an ultra-wide-angle sensor. It uses data from all the three lenses while shooting a video. iPhone 11 Pro is one of the best when it comes to video shooting capabilities.

Galaxy S20+#

The Galaxy S20+ is one of the most stocked up premium Android phone you can buy, and there’s a lot of features that take ages to explore. Inside is the new Exynos 990 coupled with up to 16GB RAM and 512GB storage. The S20 Ultra also boasts of a 108MP primary camera coupled with a 48MP periscope telephoto camera along with an ultrawide and ToF 3D sensor. On the front, you get what is perhaps the most well-tuned display measuring 6.9-inches with QHD+ resolution and HDR10+ certification.

These factors and plethora of other features including the ways one can benefit from the S-pen earns Galaxy Note10+ the 3rd spot on the list of best mobile phones in India.

The OnePlus 8 Pro is powered by the Snapdragon 865 and rocks not one, but two 48MP cameras at the back combined with another telephoto lens. It also boasts of a gorgeous 120Hz QHD+ AMOLED display and is quite ergonomic to hold. What makes this one of the great smartphones to buy right now is the stable and smooth software experience.

Huawei P30 Pro#

The Huawei P30 Pro is a camera beast that sets an example for mobile photography. It also offers flagship performance making it one of the best phones to buy right now. The low light capabilities without using a specific shooting mode is unparalleled. The 5x telephoto lens is one of a kind and would be of great interest to anyone who’s an avid traveler.

Xiaomi Mi 10#

The Xiaomi Mi 10 is the company’s first flagship smartphone in India in years. That includes the flagship Snapdragon 865, up to 8GB LPDDR5 RAM and 256GB UFS 3.0 storage. The phone also flaunts a bright and vibrant 6.67-inch AMOLED display with HDR10+ support. An attractive part of the phone is the 108MP camera at the back which is combined with another ultrawide lens, a macro lens and a depth sensor. The phone also supports 30W fast wireless charging and comes with a 4,500mAh battery.

The iQOO 3 5G is powered by the flagship Snapdragon 865 making it really fast than all other Android flagships at present. There’s also a 48MP quad camera setup at the back and another selfie camera drilled into the display.

The ROG Phone III is a device focused on a small set of high-intensity mobile gamers. The ROG Phone III is powered by the Snapdragon 865+ and comes with up to 12GB LPDDR5 RAM and 256GB UFS 3.0 storage, along with a 144Hz display and AirTriggers 3.0 which can now be split in two, to map two functions. There’s a lot going in the ROG Phone III and most of the features are tailored to make one thing great – Gaming.

The OnePlus Nord is priced under Rs 30,000, but the experience is just as smooth and fast as the OnePlus 8, a much more expensive smartphone.  There are also over 300 software optimisations in place, and combined with a 90Hz display, using the OnePlus Nord is a charm in itself.
