Item Updates – Locket Enchant#

Similar to champions, there are some changes made to the locket enchant in Wild Rift 2.1b yama notlari (patch notes). Since the locket is extremely powerful as a first product and nullifies the effectiveness of early game team battle, the developers are making a substantial cut to its early strength and price to discourage it from being obtained too fast. Locket Enchant

Price: 500g → 800g Shield Amount: 140 to 420 (120 + Level × 20) → 70 to 420 (45 + Level × 25)

Rune Updates – Conqueror#

Riot discovered that Conqueror outperforms other options on ranged AP Wild Rift champions after analyzing user data. Aside from that, they believe it is marginally powerful in general for other ranged champions, so they decided to selectively nerf its overall performance on ranged champions by a tiny proportion.

AP per stack: 4 to 12 (based on champ level) → 3 to 9 (based on champ level) Maximum stack Adaptive Damage bonus: 10% on ranged champions → 7% on ranged champions

Systems#

Apart from making changes to the champions and items, Riots also implemented some system changes with the new Wild Rift 2.1b yama notlari (patch notes). The changes are made to the following systems.

Backdoor Bonus#

While the developers agree that backdooring is most feasible in some instances, they also voiced concerns that late-game backdooring has become too convenient. As a result, they eliminated the features in the game that decreases the turret defense backdoor bonus.

“Backdoor bonus” tower damage lessens: 66%, then 33% at 18 minutes → 66% during the entire game

Lane Swaps#

The developers claim that the excessive lane switches in Wild Rift to compel 2v1 lanes in competitive play is an unhealthy game flow. As a response, in order to retain simple transitions of the game, they are offering turrets more defensive strength if a whole team attempts to take them down, making the laning last at least several minutes in the early part of the game. Before patch 2.1b

  1. During the first 3 minutes, every outer turrets receive 50 bonus Armor/Magic Resist
  2. During the first 3 minutes, turrets receive extra defensive bonuses when multiple enemies are nearby:

3 champions in total: 40 Armor/Magic Resist 4 champions in total: 80 Armor/Magic Resist 5 champions in total: 120 Armor/Magic Resist

After Patch 2.1b changes

  1. During the first 3 minutes:

Solo (Baron) lane outer turret receives 90 bonus Armor/Magic Resist Mid lane outer turret receives 90 bonus Armor/Magic Resist Duo (Dragon) lane outer turret receives 40 bonus Armor/Magic Resist

  1. During the first 3 minutes, turrets receive extra defensive bonuses when multiple enemies are nearby:

2 champions in total: 10 Armor/Magic Resist 3 champions in total: 100 Armor/Magic Resist 4 champions in total: 200 Armor/Magic Resist 5 champions in total: 300 Armor/Magic Resist

Chat Detection#

A player may encounter other players who have received text or speech constraints with the incorporation of enhanced Wild Rift chat evaluation in 2.1b yama notlari (patch notes), which amps up to identify the maximum of detected languages. The developers will continue to strengthen both the detection and the languages that Wild Rift supports in the following days.
