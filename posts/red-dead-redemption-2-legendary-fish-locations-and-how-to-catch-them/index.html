Doing the opposite will actively reduce your success rate. Best way to catch Legendary Fish As mentioned above, reeling in Legendary Fish is not easy and takes much longer per catch. This means there’s a greater chance of the line snapping, so you’ll really need to be vigilant if you want to ensure your catch isn’t temporarily lost. To do this, you’ll need to use the ‘advanced reeling-in technique’. So when a Legendary Fish is tired and no longer thrashing in the water, tilt the left analog stick back to pull your rod up, then quickly reel in with the right analog stick (spin the stick clockwise). Repeat this every time the fish rests as it will draw the fish in a little faster and give you an edge in each watery duel. Try and find a hand position for the reeling that suits you - Legendary Fish battles can last a few minutes, so try using the pads of you middle and index finger to rotate the stick faster and more consistently.
