Take a close look at the Finishing Mastery and Assist Mastery objective sets. In Finishing Mastery, you can earn a Rare Players Pack (worth 50k coins) by scoring 100 finesse goals, and you can unlock a Jumbo Rare Players Pack (worth 100k) by scoring 500 goals with a squad rating of 85+. Then over in Assist Mastery, there’s a Jumbo Rare Players Pack available for assisting 500 times with a squad rated 75+. The great thing about these objectives is that you can complete them in any FUT game mode on any difficulty setting, including ‘Beginner’ friendlies. As with the bronze and silver objective sets above, assemble a squad that fits the requirements, then head into ‘Couch Play’ friendlies, hit L2/LT to go to Game Settings, and choose ‘Beginner’ difficulty. If you are struggling with the squad rating requirements, remember that loan cards do not get used up by friendlies, so you can use your 91-rated Mbappe loan from Moments mode to boost the rating. Then simply load into a match in any friendly type you like, and begin scoring as many goals as you can with finesse shots. Make sure to complete a pass before scoring (not always the case if you regain possession from AI kickoff) to add in an assist towards that objective. It is possible to score 30-40 goals per match against the lowest AI difficulty, so it will still take a few hours to get these done, but the extra players, especially walkouts, that you can expect to see from Jumbo Rare Players Pack, could well make it worthwhile if you are struggling to put together a competitive team. At the very least, you will end up with a lot of handy fodder for Squad-Building Challenges.
