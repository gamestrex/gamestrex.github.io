Overwatch League 2022: Everything You Need To Know#

Start Date & Schedule For Overwatch League 2022#

  The complete schedule for the Overwatch League 2022 has been announced. The event is scheduled to kick off on May 5, 2022. The Schedule for the event will include:

Kickoff Clash: May-Jun Midseason Madness: Jun-Jul Summer Showdown: Aug-Sept Countdown Cup: Sept-Oct Playoffs: Oct-Nov

For a detailed breakdown of the complete schedule, players can visit the Overwatch League Match Schedule. Here, players can get a look at all the matches scheduled for the event.

Tickets For OWL 2022#

Those interested in getting tickets for the event and enjoying the experience first hand are in luck. Tickets for Battle for Texas (May 6) and The West region Kickoff Clash tournament (June 2-5) are now available for purchase from Blizzard’s official site. Readers can just click here to go to the Tickets page. Below are the Ticket details for each event.

Battle for Texas: Houston Outlaws vs Dallas Fuel#

Event Date: May 6, 2022 5:00 PM Event Venue: Tech Port Arena Ticket Cost: $39.99 (General Admission), $125 (VIP)

Kickoff Clash#

Event Date: Jun 2 – 5 Event Venue: Esports Stadium Arlington & Expo Center, Arlington, TX Ticket Cost: $99 (General Admission), $129-$229 (VIP)
