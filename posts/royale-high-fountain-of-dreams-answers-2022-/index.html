Fountain Of Dreams Answers In Royale High#

When players walk to the Fountain of Dreams, they get the option to ‘Make a wish’ or ‘Walk away’. Players that choose to make a wish will get a mini-story where players need to choose from four possible outcomes. Depending on the answer they choose, players can either get diamonds or experience. They can also lose diamonds as a result of certain choices. We have put together the best answers that players can choose to win rewards at the Fountain of Dreams in Royale High.

Choose A Rose From The Table#

Option C is the safest bet here. It will either give diamonds and XP or nothing will happen. Players do not stand to lose anything in this option. Option B will either give a rare halo, XP or diamonds to players but they can also lose diamonds with this. On the other hand, Option A can give players diamonds, XP, or nothing. It can also take away diamonds from players. Those that choose Option D will either get XP or Diamonds. Conversely, they can also lose diamonds with this choice.

Activity Stands In The Magical Realm#

No choice is really safe for this storyline. Players can lose diamonds with any of the options. Still, Option A gives players the chance to win XP, Halo or nothing. Players opting for Option B or D can win diamonds or XP. Those that go for Option C can win diamonds, XP or a Halo.

Crying Cupid#

Option D is that safest with either diamonds or nothing happening. Players can lose diamonds in all the other choices. Option A will either yield XP or diamonds. Alternatively, those that opt for Option B can get XP, diamonds or nothing. Players going for Option C can get diamonds, XP, Halo or nothing.

Teddyzilla Explodes Pastry Chef Ashe’s Desserts#

Penguin Looking For The Smoothest Stone#

Busy Cupid#
