How Do I Use Scum Admin Commands?#

It is super easy to enter admin Console Commands, making it a lot easier to get your hands on the desired item. To use Scum admin commands, you simply need to hit the T button to bring up the text chat box, put in a hashtag, and then enter the command string followed by the desired item ID. For better understanding, let’s take an example. If you want to get an MP5, you will have to hit T and enter #Spawnitem bd_weapon_mp5 followed by the Enter key. You will need to hit the TAB button to spot the item on the nearby floor.

Scum Admin Commands#

There are plenty of Admin commands that you can enter in Scum. We have compiled a list of all commands and what they do in the game.

#LIST#

Provides a full list of Scum admin commands, in case you forget something #list

#LISTPLAYERS#

Lists all players in the server, which is useful for the next couple of commands #listplayers

#KICK#

Kicks a specific player from your server #kick playername

#BAN#

Permanently bans a specific player from your server #ban playername

#LOCATION#

Gives you the exact location of a specific player #location playername

#LISTASSETS#

Provides a list of all assets in the game, can be filtered to find particular items #listassets wood

#SPAWNITEM#

Spawns a select number of a specific item right in front of you #spawnitem cal_9mm

#SPAWNCHARACTER#

Spawns a specific NPC, animal, or type of puppet directly in front of you #spawncharacter bp_cow2

#TELEPORT#

Teleports you to the desired coordinates #teleport 143,1222,1333

#TELEPORTTOPLAYER#

Teleports you to a specific character #teleport playername

#SETFAMEPOINTS#

Sets your Fame Points to the value given #setfamepoints 400 playername
