Genshin Impact Phantasmal Conches explained#

There are 20 Phantasmal Conches to collect during the Resonating Visions event in Genshin Impact. You will receive Ascension materials, Mora, and Primogems for collecting different amounts of Conches, and a new Fischl skin is available for free by collecting 16 Phantasmal Conches.

Phantasmal Conches explained

Phantasmal Conches locations:

All 20 Phantasmal Conches can be found in the Golden Apple Archipelago collection of islands, although only 14 Conches can be found without completing the Summertime Odyssey domain event quests. To get to the Golden Apple Archipelago islands, you need to start the Summertime Odyssey event from the Event menu. To do this, you need to be at least Adventure Rank 32, and have completed the Archon Quest ‘Chapter II: Prologue - Autumn Winds, Scarlet Leaves’. Follow the quest steps until your ragtag party arrives at the islands during the Event Quest ‘Summertime Odyssey I: The Golden Apple Vacation Returns!’ Once you arrive on the first island, you’re then free to explore the rest and search for the first 14 Phantasmal Conches. To gain access to the other six Conches, you’ll need to continue the Summertime Odyssey event quest and complete Kazuha, Xinyan, Fischl, and Mona’s domains. There’s a Conch located in each domain, and two more on Twinning Isle and Broken Isle - but you need to solve a puzzle for each. You’ll have until Tuesday, 23rd August to find Phantasmal Conches and claim your rewards. After this, the islands won’t be available to explore. We recommend bringing Qiqi or Yanfei while searching for Conches so you can take advantage of their very helpful passive talents that enable them to detect collectibles and materials, which are displayed on your minimap. Genshin Impact Minacious Isle Phantasmal Conches locations Day 1#

There are three Phantasmal Conches locations all close to each other on Minacious Isle, the southernmost island in the Golden Apple Archipelago.

Minacious Island Phantasmal Conches location 1#

The first Phantasmal Conch on Minacious Island can be found on a rock underneath the middle of the cliffs to the west of the island.

Minacious Island Phantasmal Conches location 2#

You can find the second Minacious Isle Conch atop the inner cliffs by the first Conch location. You’ll have to climb the tree roots growing on the inside of this area, and jump and climb on the crumbling ruins inside to reach its location.

Minacious Isle Phantasmal Conches location 3#

The final Conch on Minacious Isle is located at the very top of the western cliffs you were climbing to get to the second Conch location. There is a ladder directly opposite the second Conch that leads to the top of the cliff and this third Conch.

Genshin Impact Pudding Isle Phantasmal Conches locations Day 1#

There are three Phantasmal Conches locations on Pudding Isle, the island to the southeast of the Golden Apple Archipelago.

Pudding Isle Phantasmal Conches location 1#

The first Phantasmal Conch on Pudding Isle can be found on a small rock by the coastline on the southwestern side of the island.

Pudding Isle Phantasmal Conches location 2#

You can find the second Pudding Isle Conch by the eastern coastline, on a small rock.

Pudding Isle Phantasmal Conches location 3#

The final Conch on Pudding Isle is located right beside the Teleport Waypoint on top of the island’s eastern cliff. You’ll have to use tree roots and time your climbing breaks right to reach its location.

Genshin Impact Twinning Isle Phantasmal Conches locations Day 1#

There are four Phantasmal Conches locations on and around Twinning Isle, the island to the northeast of the Golden Apple Archipelago.

Twinning Isle Phantasmal Conches location 1#

The first Phantasmal Conch on Twinning Isle can be found on a rock near the southern coastline of the first small island to the east of the Waverider Waypoint.

Twinning Isle Phantasmal Conches location 2#

You can find the second Twinning Isle Conch on the northern island, on a hill near the western coast.

Twinning Isle Phantasmal Conches location 3#

The third Conch on Twinning Isle is located on top of the middle cliff of the northern island, by some wooden debris.

Twinning Isle Phantasmal Conches location 4#

The final Conch by Twinning Isle is located on a wooden crate, right in the middle of the smallest island, to the southwest. Genshin Impact Broken Isle Phantasmal Conches locations Day 1#

There are four Phantasmal Conches locations on Broken Isle, the large island to the northwest of the Golden Apple Archipelago.

Broken Isle Phantasmal Conches location 1#

The first Phantasmal Conch on Broken Isle can be found on the southeastern side of the small island to the east. It’s on a rocky area by an enemy camp and the ‘Investigate’ prompt glowing gold.

Broken Isle Phantasmal Conches location 2#

You can find the second Broken Isle Conch by the northern coast of the largest island, just southwest of the small island containing the first Conch location.

Broken Isle Phantasmal Conches location 3#

The third Conch on Broken Isle is located near the northwestern coast of the largest island, on top of a small rocky area.

Broken Isle Phantasmal Conches location 4#

The final Conch on Broken Isle is located between the two cliffs on the western side of the largest island in the area. It’s on a large rock by the dirt path.

Genshin Impact Phantasmal Conches Day 2#

There was only one Phantasmal Conch location added during Day 2 of the Summertime Odyssey event. You can find it in Kazuha’s domain, ‘Misty Hills, Foggy Vales’, either during his ‘As the Courtyard in Spring’ event quest, or by visiting the domain again once the quest is over. You can find Kazuha’s domain Conch near the end of the area, when you’re traversing floating buildings by creating wind tunnels. It’s inside a building up a wooden staircase, with a cherry blossom tree at the top of the stairs.

Genshin Impact Phantasmal Conches Day 3#

There were two Phantasmal Conch locations added during Day 3 of the Summertime Odyssey event. You can find one in Xinyan’s domain, ‘The Islands’ Stirring Strings’, either during her ‘Blazin’ Trails’ event quest, or by visiting the domain again once the quest is over. The second Day 3 Conch is located inside a large Broken Isle cave, but it can only be accessed by completing the ‘Blazin’ Trails’ quest first.

‘Blazin’ Trails’ Xinyan domain Phantasmal Conch location#

You can find Xinyan’s domain Conch in a bonus area behind an orange door. This door can be found in the middle area of the caves above the talking flower, just past the tunnel with the large sparkling blue flower. The Conch is then at the back of this bonus area. You need to create wind tunnels with the music notes to get there. Once you arrive at the back, rotate the devices so the large device creates a wind tunnel when a music note hits it. Use this wind tunnel to fly to the cliff opposite it and pick up the Phantasmal Conch.

Broken Isle Phantasmal Conch location Day 3#

Go to the drum where you enter ‘The Islands’ Stirring Strings’ domain from, and instead pick ‘Change mountain style’. This will lower the size of the mountain on your right. When this mountain is lowered, enter it from the northern side. Switch to a Pyro character and light up the torches surrounding the Pyro elemental pillar, and then you can light up the pillar itself. Head through to the next area and light up the torch on the left-hand side of the cave, then the second one beside the Pyro pillar at the back, the third one on the left-hand side of the wooden stairs past the Pyro pillar, and then go through the door at the top to light the fourth and final torch. You can now head back to the Pyro pillar and activate it. The water in this area will then drain, and you can now pick up the Broken Isle Day 3 Conch opposite the chest in the middle.

Genshin Impact Phantasmal Conches Day 4#

There were two Phantasmal Conch locations added during Day 4 of the Summertime Odyssey event. You can find one in Fischl’s domain, ‘Immernachtreich Keep’, either during her ‘Immernachtreich Apokalypse’ event quest, or by visiting the domain again once the quest is over. The other Day 4 Phantasmal Conch is located on the northern island of Twinning Isle after completing ‘Immernachtreich Apokalypse’, but you’ll need to solve the stone raven puzzle first.

‘Immernachtreich Keep’ Fischl domain Phantasmal Conch location#

The Phantasmal Conch in Fischl’s domain is located at the start of the area where you had to use the Night Raven’s sight camera to solve the stairs puzzle with your view blocked for the first time. If searching for the Conch during the story, this is after the party has a conversation about Fischl and dragons, after they have been transported here. Your marked quest will ask you to ‘Operate the device to repair the path’. Create a path up the stairs by using the Night Raven’s sight camera and kill all the enemies on this walkway and the small area beyond. Once the enemies have been defeated, you can pick up the Conch located on the wooden table by the back wall of this small area.

Twinning Isle Phantasmal Conch location Day 4#

After completing Fischl’s domain during the ‘Immernachtreich Apokalypse’ event quest, you can visit the northern island of Twinning Isle to solve the stone raven puzzle to find the next Phantasmal Conch. Talk to the three stone ravens surrounding the well to get the ‘Throw Stone’ prompt to appear and click it. Choose three eligible stones that aren’t blacked-out to throw them into the well. The Conch will then float to the top of the well and you can now click it to add it to your collection!

Genshin Impact Phantasmal Conches Day 5#

The final Phantasmal Conch location of the Resonating Visions event quest is located inside Mona’s domain, ‘Temple of the Star Latitudes’. You can pick it up while in the ‘Ancient Azure Stars’ event quest, or by revisiting the domain later. The Phantasmal Conch inside Mona’s domain is located behind the first door you entered during the story, to the right of the Resurrection Door. You’ll need to have solved the Astral puzzle to summon the chest in order to open the door up the stairs to the back of the room to gain access to the next area. This next area will have another Astral puzzle to solve. Input the three correct patterns that appear on the wall in order for the wall constellation to change to an arrow. Go to the wall the arrow is pointing at and touch it to reveal the next area. Follow the astral path up and into a new puzzle room with four round switches on the floor. The Phantasmal conch is located on the left-hand side of these switches. Just pick it up to complete your collection and the Resonating Visions event quest! Remember to claim all of your Resonating Visions rewards - including the free Fischl skin - from the Event Menu.
