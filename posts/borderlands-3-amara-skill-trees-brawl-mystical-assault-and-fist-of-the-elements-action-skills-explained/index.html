Borderlands 3 Amara Skill Trees Brawl Mystical Assault And Fist Of The Elements Action Skills Explained

November 23, 2022 · 6 min · 1134 words · Katherine Rothstein

In Borderlands 3, Amara joins the ranks of the Siren class, alongside Lilith and Maya. Born in the slums of Partali, Amara learn how to channel her Siren energy into a set of powerful arms that she can now use to destroy her enemies. She’s a woman of action and her home is on the battlefield. On this page: Spending Skill Points in the Skill Trees will allow you to unlock a variety of Action Skill Effects. These will allow you to add an extra feature to your chosen Action Skill. You can have 1 Action Skill Effect equipped. The Skill Trees will also allow you to unlock Action Skill Elements. These will convert the type of damage that your Action Skill deals. When you first begin playing as Amara, the Action Skill Element Shockra will already be equipped. This will convert your Action Skill damage into Shock Damage. You can have 1 Action Skill Element equipped at a time. There is a special focus on Melee Damage, with abilities like Find Your Center, which will increase your Melee Damage. If you decide to reach the end of this Skill Tree, you’ll unlock Blitz. This will override your set Melee attack with a far more powerful version. Brawl will also help you stay alive for longer on the battlefield by allowing you to increase your Maximum Health through Abilities like Root to Rise and One with Nature. The focus on Melee Damage returns with the Action Skill. Phaseslam is a powerful move that will allow you to slam enemies, knocking them into the air. Throughout the Skill Tree you can unlock new version of this Action Skill, which deal Elemental Damage. There are also a number of Action Skill Effects that will allow to up the damage Phaseslam can bring to the battlefield. This includes Revelation, which always you to create a Nova when using Phaseslam on your enemies. Action skill for the Brawl Skill Tree - Phaseslam - leap into the air and slam into the ground

Phasecast is the main Action Skill for Mystical Assault and allows you to send an Astral Projection of Amara out onto the battlefield. You’ll be able to unlock new versions of this Action Skill that will let you use the Astral Projection to deal more damage, Reverberation, and release Elemental Projectiles when it hits an enemy, Deliverance. You can also add Action Skill Effects to Phasecast. Soul Sap will let you transform some of the damage dealt by Phasecast into health, which is very useful in long boss battles. Two of Amara’s Action Skills are very reminiscent of the past Sirens. Phasegrasp, which allows you to grab hold of oncoming enemies, is very similar to Maya’s Phaselock, which suspends enemies in a vortex. Amara’s Phasecast Action Skill is a more violent version of Lilith’s Phasewalk. At a glance, it appears that Amara is just a combined version of the past Sirens, however, the Brawl Skill Tree offers to makes her incredibly deadly in close combat. Include the possibility to inflict a great amount of elemental damage upon your enemies and Amara is shaping up to be the strongest Siren Borderlands has seen yet.
