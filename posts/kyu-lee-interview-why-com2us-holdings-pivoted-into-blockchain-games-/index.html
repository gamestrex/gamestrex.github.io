Kyu Lee Interview Why Com2Us Holdings Pivoted Into Blockchain Games

November 28, 2022 · 21 min · 4427 words · Colleen Pace

In 2016, the Seoul-based company invested in a big cryptocurrency exchange in South Korea in the year 2020 and was invested in the Wysiwyg studio. I have hoped for partners and invested in Animoca, Sandbox, Upland and Mythical Games. They changed its name to Com2Us Holdings and Gamevil. That company is going to be pivoting in a major way through free-to-play games that have brought great prosperity and partnerships with brands like the NBA. The entire family is entering NFT and other blockchain technologies. You’ll always be aware of that, especially if you take these types of things like it, and focus on them, in the same way that it focused many years ago on playing free games. It makes Com2Us one of the biggest mobile game companies to move into NFT. Since it was founded in 1998 and has a son in South Korea, Com2uS became a subsidiary of mobile game developer Gamevil in 2013 (and now the whole company is Com2Us Holdings). Com2Us Summoners War has more than 100 million downloads and sales worth $2 billion. The upcoming Com2U games are Chromatic Soul: AFK, Kritika: the White Knights, Project MR, and Summoners War: Chronicles. Baseball Superstars, World of Zenonia and The Great Merchant will also be available soon. I caught up with Lee about this pivot, and gave him an edited transcript of the interview. He was always thinking about the metamorphic stuff. Between them, we acquired a company called Wysiwyg studio. Wysiwyg Studios is a South Korean company publicly listed. They’re originally a VFX studio. They have done lots of things in the metaverse preparation field, so to speak. However, when we bought them, we did it because we thought that Netflix was the new app store for all of the television producers in Korea. Terra is a South Korean company, an established crypto company. This means they have an amazing ecosystem. I was very shocked they weren’t as well-known in the West. They have always gained traction in the past few months, but they have still been very popular.
