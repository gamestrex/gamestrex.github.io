MIR4 Server Down: How To Fix MIR4 Connection Failed Issue in October 2021?#

Whenever you face a MIR4 connection failure issue on your PC and Mobile, the first thing that you must do is to check whether the server of MIR4 is down or not. Since the devs of MIR4 were supposed to drop a new update today, there are chances that this issue is might be happening from their end. To check MIR4 server status, you simply need to go to the official Twitter or Discord handle of MIR4 where they keep their fans informed about the latest development. Besides their official site or social media handles, you can also check the server status of MIR4 on a third-party site called downdetector.  If the ‘MIR4 connection failed’ issue that you are encountering right now is not from their end then you will have to apply the following methods to get it fixed.

Check Your Internet Connection#

If you think that the error that you are currently having on MIR4 is from your end then the first thing that you must do is check your Internet connection. If you are using Wi-Fi to play MIR4 then I advise you to restart your router or if you are using your Internet data to play MIR4 on your phone then reboot your device to get the issue fixed.

Update MIR4 App#

If you still are facing a MIR4 connection failed issue on your device then check to make sure the version that you are currently using is up to date. If you play MIR4 on your phone, go to Google Play Store and Apple App Store and search for ‘MIR4’. If the game is not updated, you will get the option ‘Update’ to download the latest version of the game. Once updated, launch MIR4 on your phone and see if the error remains.

Clear Cache#

Before you update MIR4 via Google Play Store and Apple App Store, I strictly advise you to clear your app data and cache by going to the Settings>Apps and Notifications>Select MIR4>Storage and Cache. Once cleared, now you can go to Play Store and download the latest version of the game.

Uninstall MIR4 & Restart Your Phone#

If you still are having a MIR4 connection failed issue on your device then you have no option left but to uninstall and reinstall the game on your device. Before you reinstall the game on your Android and iOS devices, restart your device. Once restarted, go to Google Play Store and Apple App Store and download and install the game again. Once installed, launch the game and check if the error is fixed or not. If none of the above-mentioned methods helped you to fix the MIR4 connection failed issue, you will have to wait until the server is up again. Once the server is up, you would not face any issues while playing the game.
