Everything New Available With The Splitgate Season 0 Battle Pass#

Here is a list of all the new cosmetics, items, new game mode, and new map in Splitgate.

The first and foremost is the new Karman station map. The map is a modified version of the “Outpost” map and with the release of the Karman station map, the outpost map will not be available in the game. A new Contamination mode is also added in Splitgate. The mode is a classic asymmetrical multiplayer mode where your team is up against a smaller contaminated team. Every time a player from your team dies he is resurrected in the contaminated mode making the loss double. Apart from these, Battle Pass with 100 levels have been introduced, this means players will have more cosmetics, weapons, skins, and currency to obtain.

Splitgate Season 0 Start Date And End Date#

Season 0  of Splitgate was released on 25th August and it is live now. As of now, there is no official announcement regarding the end date of the Splitgte Season 0. We are expecting Season 0 to last anywhere between 45 days to 60 days. The Battle Pass is expected to end in the final week of October, this is just speculation and nothing is official as of now.
