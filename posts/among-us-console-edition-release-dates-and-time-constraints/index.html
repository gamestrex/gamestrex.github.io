Console Edition is available by means of time and time.#

The PlayStation 4 version of Among Us comes to PlayStation 5, PlayStation 5, and Xbox X|S on Tuesday, December 14th 2021. According to the PlayStation Store page for the Among Us Console Edition, the release time is 7PM GMT and that means a lot less than the US’s 11pm PT / 2PM ET. What’s new in the Among Us Console Edition?#

If you already own a copy of Among Us from PC or Mobile Devices, then you may wonder if there’s a reason to purchase a copy on either PlayStation or Xbox. Nintendo and Among Us console will get an exclusive Ratchet & Clank Cosmetic set for use in-game. The set is made with a full Ratchet outfit and a Clank-piped. There are no major differences in the PC and mobile versions. In addition to Among Us, the console will support crossplay. If you want to play with friends on PC, Mobile or Switch, you’ll be able to do it without it. For more details on Among Us Console, please visit the PlayStation and Xbox Store page.
