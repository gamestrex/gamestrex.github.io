Minimum Requirements to Play Diablo Immortal#

Before buying a compatible smartphone to play Diablo Immortal, just keep these specs in mind.

For android devices Processor (CPU) – Snapdragon 660 / Exynos 9611 and higher. GPU – Adreno 512 / Mali-G62 MP3 and higher. RAM – 2GB of RAM and higher. Version: Android OS 5.0 (Lollipop) and higher. For iOS devices iPhone 6s and newer. Version: iOS 11 and higher. iPadOS 11.0 or later.
