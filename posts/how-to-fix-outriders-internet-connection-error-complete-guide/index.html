How To Fix Outrider Internet Connection Error?#

Unfortunately, there are thousands of Outriders players who reported that they are having internet connection errors. It has become quite normal for upcoming titles to give players a hard time before allowing them to experience a smooth ride. If you are one such player-facing connection issue then here’s how to fix the Outrider internet connection error.

Restart System#

Viruses, as well as hardware and software issues, can corrupt your system which results in several in-game errors. Besides many apps and process running in the background also affects the system’s performance. Hence, to get rid of all the unnecessary processes running in the background and clear the system’s cache as well restart your device. This de-corrupts your system’s memory as well kills the background processes rendering your device a new start. With this easy fix, you can now play the game devoid of any errors, glitches, and bugs.

Check Your Internet Connection#

An unstable internet connection may give you trouble in running Outriders seamlessly. To overcome the network issue simply switch off your modem and restart after 10 sec. This restores the signal and rebuilds the connection which ultimately addresses the issue. Another way is to disconnect Wi-Fi from idle devices to reduce the network load or simply switch to a wired connection. Opting for a wired connection is the best thing since it delivers a fast and durable net. For this plugin, the Ethernet cable to your device’s port and you are all set.

Configure NAT Type And Qos Settings#

One reason for the game giving you hard time can be incorrect router settings. For online gaming NAT type and proper bandwidth allocation plays an important role. Just modify the router’s advanced settings. Check out how to configure NAT type and Qos settings below: Configure NAT Type NAT type is a gateway between your IP and game datacentres. Strict NAT type means unsecure connection and to fix it follow the below steps:

Step 1: Open the router settings menu. Step 2: Search for the UPnP option. Step 3: Enable UPnP. Step 4: Restart your router.

Hopefully doing so may fix the Outriders internet connection error. Also Read | Outriders Split Screen Local Multiplayer Configure QoS QoS prioritizes the running process on your IP by offering a particular app with higher bandwidth as compared to others. Hence, you can deliver the Outrider with higher bandwidth so that it can easily connect to the game’s server. To fix it follow the below steps:

Step 1: Open the router settings menu. Step 2: Search for QoS. Step 3: Enable QoS. Step 4: Look for Outriders in processes. Step 5: Set Outriders to a higher priority. Step 6: Restart your router.

This will offer the shooter title higher bandwidth and resolves the issue. It is however highly recommended to opt for a 5GHz router for a seamless experience since they are fastest, secured, and highly stable. Also, make sure to connect with your service provider for configuration purposes since settings may be different for different routers.

Install Google DNS#

Slower DNS discourages the game servers to identify your address which leads to a server connection error. Switching to Google DNS can address the issue since it is quick, highly stable as well as secured. To switch to Google DNS simply follow the below steps:

Step 1: Open Control Panel. Step 2: Go to Network and Internet settings. Step 3: Search for DNS options. Step 4: Type 8.8.8.8 in the primary tab. Step 5: Type 8.8.8.4 in the secondary tab. Step 6: Save the settings. Step 7: Restart the router.

Upon switching to Google DNS you are not likely to encounter any more server connection issues.

Reinstall Outriders#

This will be the last option to resolve the error. There may be some issue from the server sides which has something to do with the game’s downloaded files. Simply uninstall the game as well as discard the downloaded files. The glitch in the files may trigger the system error hence once removed from the desktop reinstall the game. That’s all the possible ways you can resolve the Outriders internet connection error. However, if unlucky you still face the issue then all you can do is wait patiently for the developers to fix the issue themselves.
